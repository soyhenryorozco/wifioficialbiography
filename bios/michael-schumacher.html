Michael Schumacher (born January 3, 1969), known as Michael Schumacher, is a german formula one driver. Born in Hürth, Germany, Michael Schumacher won seven Formula One World Championships, a record that stood for nearly two decades, and is considered one of the greatest drivers ever.
