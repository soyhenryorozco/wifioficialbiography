Elon Musk (born June 28, 1971), known as Elon Musk, is a south african-canadian-american ceo of tesla and spacex. Born in Pretoria, South Africa, Elon Musk is the CEO of Tesla, SpaceX, and X (formerly Twitter). He is one of the wealthiest people in the world and a pioneer in electric vehicles and space exploration.
