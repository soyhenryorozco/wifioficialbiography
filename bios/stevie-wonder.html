Stevie Wonder (born May 13, 1950), known as Stevie Wonder, is a american singer and songwriter. Born in Saginaw, Michigan, USA, Stevie Wonder is a musical genius with 25 Grammy Awards, known for classics like Superstition and Isn't She Lovely.
