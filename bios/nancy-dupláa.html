Nancy Dupláa (born August 3, 1969), known as Nancy Dupláa, is a argentine actress. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Nancy Dupláa is an Argentine actress known for telenovelas and films. She is one of Argentina's most recognized television actresses.
