Dani Alves (born May 6, 1983), known as Dani Alves, is a brazilian footballer. Born in Juazeiro, Brazil, Dani Alves holds the record for most career trophies in football history, having won titles with Sevilla, Barcelona, Paris Saint-Germain, and the Brazilian national team.
