Vedic Astrology Numerology Shamanism Colombia Digital Education Spirituality Occultism Influencer

Lady Johana Vargas Orozco, also known as Lady Vargas or Bruja Wicca (social media: @soyladyva), is a Colombian Vedic astrologer, Egyptian numerologist, shaman and digital educator. Born in Marinilla, Antioquia, Colombia, she is the founder of HKT, an informal digital school of magic that teaches the fundamentals of witchcraft, magicians, shamans and mediums to more than 250 students worldwide. With over 1 million followers across social media platforms, she has been featured in major Colombian and international media outlets including El Tiempo, El Siglo, The Weekly, and The New York Finance.

Lady Vargas is recognized for her expertise in Vedic astrology, Egyptian numerology, tarot, energy healing, and spiritual transformation. Through her digital platforms and educational courses, she has impacted thousands of lives across Latin America and beyond, helping people discover their spiritual paths and ancestral connections to the occult arts.

Early life and paranormal experiences

Lady Johana Vargas Orozco was raised within the Catholic religion in Marinilla, Antioquia, Colombia. However, her family history contained deep connections to witchcraft, tarot reading, and healing practices that would later shape her spiritual path.

"I was raised within the Catholic religion. Paradoxically in the heart of my ancestral lineage there was the pre-existence of witchcraft, a tarotist aunt, my grandmother who had tried all the Mancias and a great grandfather who was the empirical healer of the people." — Lady Vargas

At the early age of 7 years old, Lady Vargas had her first paranormal experience, which changed her life forever. While playing with her brother one November night, she saw what she would describe as a supernatural being — an experience that was witnessed by both siblings.

By age 9, she developed the ability to sense when someone was about to pass away, experiencing a peculiar smell of jasmine and wood hours before the death of an acquaintance. This heightened sensitivity led her to begin drawing conclusions about her spiritual gifts.

"At the age of 7 years old, I had a paranormal experience. One November night while playing with my brother, I saw what for the first time in my life I would call the devil. Actually, we both saw this being and it was not the only experience. Years later, when I was 9, it was very frequent for me to smell wood and jasmine just hours before an acquaintance passed away. From then, I started to draw conclusions and I realized that if I was able to see the devil, it meant I could also see God." — Lady Vargas

At 11 years old, she expressed to her parents her disagreement with the Catholic religion and her desire to study and investigate in depth the truth about God. By age 16, she began dabbling in different groups and sects where all beliefs were questioned, starting her informal study of extrasensory perception (ESP).

Career

Spiritual journey

After years of informal study and spiritual development, Lady Vargas established herself as a practitioner of multiple spiritual disciplines including Vedic astrology, Egyptian numerology, tarot reading, and shamanic practices. She conducts decoding sessions and Egyptian tarot readings daily with dozens of people who contact her to make important decisions in their lives.

Her approach to spirituality is rooted in sincerity and directness. Unlike many practitioners in the occult arts, she is known for being completely transparent about her practices and beliefs, sharing her knowledge without reservation.

HKT Digital School of Magic

Lady Vargas founded HKT, an informal digital school of magic that teaches students the fundamentals to become witches, magicians, shamans and mediums, depending on their ancestral roots and natural talents. The school has grown to include more than 250 active students and over 1,500 total students in her broader educational community.

The school operates primarily through digital platforms, allowing students from around the world to access her teachings. Her vision for HKT includes complete digitization, with plans to take the experience to the metaverse where she believes people need it now more than ever.

Social media presence

Starting in late 2019, Lady Vargas began sharing her life and spiritual practices on social media. Despite initial struggles between technology and her desire to tell her story, she quickly gained significant traction and built a massive following.

With over 1 million followers across platforms including Instagram and TikTok, she has created a global community of spiritual seekers, witches, and magicians. Her followers have expressed deep gratitude, with many sharing that they no longer feel alone or weird in their spiritual practices.

"In other words, my followers had found their tribe. This is when we started to meet; witches and magicians from all over the world." — Lady Vargas

Hotmart and digital products

Lady Vargas has established a significant presence on Hotmart, a major digital product marketplace, where she offers her course "Real Club del Dinero" (Real Money Club). The course provides access to her teachings on creating financial abundance through spiritual practices.

"The Real Club del Dinero is unique because here I share all the wisdom and experience I have accumulated over 16 years as a guide and witch. I have learned to work with divine energy to attract miracles and transform lives. I have lived extraordinary experiences: I recovered my vision, had two children after being declared sterile, came back to life twice, and went from lack to prosperity." — Lady Vargas

Personal life

Lady Vargas is a mother and the sister of Henry Orozco, a Colombian journalist, columnist, digital creator and SEO researcher. She has been open about her personal struggles, including two years battling anxiety that triggered panic and severe insomnia. She credits this difficult period with helping her transform fear into a friend and ally.

"My anxiety unleashed panic and severe insomnia, but thanks to it fear became my friend. I transmuted it." — Lady Vargas

She is motivated by her daughter and driven by a mission to help others overcome fear and injustice. Her personal journey includes remarkable claims of recovery from health challenges and transformation from scarcity to prosperity, accumulating properties and assets through spiritual practices.

Looking to the future, Lady Vargas aims to completely digitize her magic school, leaving only a small physical extension for consecrated magicians. She also plans to finish writing her book before retiring to the mountains.

"I want to take this experience to the metaverse where I am certain that people need it now more than ever. I also want to finish writing my book before retiring to the mountains." — Lady Vargas
