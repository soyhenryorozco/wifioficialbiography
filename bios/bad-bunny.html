Bad Bunny (born March 10, 1994), known as Bad Bunny, is a puerto rican singer. Born in Vega Baja, Puerto Rico, Bad Bunny is a Puerto Rican reggaeton and Latin trap artist who has become one of the most streamed musicians in the world. He has won multiple Grammy Awards.
