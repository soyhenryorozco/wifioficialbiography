Biography

Chris Beard (singer) is a Singer.

Chris Beard (born August 29, 1957) is an American electric blues singer, guitarist, and songwriter. He has released six albums as of 2023, the first of which was nominated for a Blues Music Award. He is dubbed 'Prince of the Blues'.
