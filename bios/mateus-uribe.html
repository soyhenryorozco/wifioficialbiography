Mateus Uribe (born March 21, 1991), known as Mateus Uribe, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Medellín, Colombia, A dynamic midfielder, Uribe played for Club América in Mexico and Porto. He has been a key figure in Colombia's midfield in recent years.
