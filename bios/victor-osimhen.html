Victor Osimhen (born December 29, 1998), known as Victor Osimhen, is a nigerian footballer. Born in Lagos, Nigeria, Victor Osimhen led Napoli to their first Serie A title in 33 years in 2023, becoming one of the most sought-after strikers in world football.
