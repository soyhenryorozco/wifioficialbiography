Édgar Ramírez (born March 25, 1977), known as Édgar Ramírez, is a venezuelan actor. Born in San Cristóbal, Venezuela, Édgar Ramírez is a Venezuelan actor known for Carlos, Zero Dark Thirty, and Gold. He is one of the most respected Latin American actors in Hollywood.
