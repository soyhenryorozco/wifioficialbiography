Chichí Peralta (born April 12, 1966), known as Chichí Peralta, is a dominican singer. Born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, Chichí Peralta is a Dominican singer and percussionist known for merengue and salsa. He has been a key figure in modern Dominican music.
