Wisin & Yandel (born July 24, 1978), known as Wisin & Yandel, is a puerto rican reggaeton duo. Born in Cayey, Puerto Rico, Wisin & Yandel are a Puerto Rican reggaeton duo known as Los Dúo de la Historia, instrumental in popularizing reggaeton globally.
