Biography

Gui França is a Footballer.

Guilherme Diniz França (born 8 July 1999) or known for short as Guilherme França or Gui França is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Campeonato Brasileiro Série D club Sampaio Corrêa.
