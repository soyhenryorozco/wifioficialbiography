Rhett and Link (born 1977-06-11), known as Rhett and Link, is a american comedy duo and youtubers. Born in Buies Creek, USA, Rhett McLaughlin and Link Neal are a comedy duo known for their YouTube show Good Mythical Morning and Mythical Entertainment.
