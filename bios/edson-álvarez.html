Edson Álvarez (born October 24, 1997), known as Edson Álvarez, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Tlalnepantla, Mexico, A defensive midfielder known for his ball-winning ability, Álvarez moved from Ajax to West Ham and became a crucial player in the Premier League.
