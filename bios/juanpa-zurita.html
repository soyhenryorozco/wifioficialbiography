Juan Pablo Martinez-Zurita Arellano (born March 29, 1996), known as Juanpa Zurita, is a Mexican YouTuber, model, and actor. Born in Ciudad de Mexico, he became famous in 2013 through comedy videos on Vine.

After Vine shut down, Zurita transitioned to YouTube, where he continues to create vlogs and entertainment content. He has also built a successful career as a model for brands like Pull and Bear and Louis Vuitton.

Zurita is known for his humanitarian campaigns and has been recognized as one of the most influential young Mexicans.
