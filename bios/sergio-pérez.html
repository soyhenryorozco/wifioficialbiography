Sergio Pérez (born January 26, 1990), known as Sergio Pérez, is a mexican formula one driver. Born in Guadalajara, Mexico, Known as 'Checo', Pérez is Mexico's most successful F1 driver with multiple race victories. He finished runner-up in the World Championship in 2023 with Red Bull Racing.
