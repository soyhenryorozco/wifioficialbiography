Andrés Pastrana Arango (born August 17, 1954) is a Colombian politician who served as President of Colombia from 1998 to 2002. Born in Bogotá, he implemented Plan Colombia.

Pastrana attempted peace negotiations with the FARC guerrilla group during his presidency.
