John Leguizamo (born July 22, 1960) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, and film producer. He has appeared in more than 100 films and produced more than 20 films and documentaries.

Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Leguizamo began his career as a stand-up comedian in New York City. He rose to fame with roles in Super Mario Bros. (1993), Carlito's Way (1993), and To Wong Foo (1995).

He is known for voicing Sid the Sloth in the Ice Age franchise and Bruno Madrigal in Encanto (2021). He received a Primetime Emmy Award for his HBO special Freak (1998).
