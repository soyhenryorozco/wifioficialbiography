Greeicy (born October 30, 1992) is a Colombian actress and singer. Born in Cali, Valle del Cauca, Colombia, she has been active in the entertainment industry since 2007.

Greeicy first gained recognition as a child on the reality show Factor Xs in 2007. She went on to star in numerous Colombian telenovels including "Chica vampiro", "La ronca de oro", and "Las Vega's". Her acting career established her as one of Colombia's most popular actresses.

In 2017, she transitioned to music with hits like "Brindemos", "Error", and "Amantes". Her music career has been commercially successful, with multiple singles reaching the top of Latin music charts. She is in a relationship with fellow Colombian singer Mike Bahía.
