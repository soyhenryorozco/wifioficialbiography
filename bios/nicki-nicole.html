Nicki Nicole (born August 25, 1999), known as Nicki Nicole, is a argentine singer. Born in Rosario, Argentina, Nicki Nicole is an Argentine rapper and singer known for blending hip-hop, pop, and trap. She is one of the biggest names in Argentine urban music.
