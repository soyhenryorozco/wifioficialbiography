Álvaro José Arroyo González (born November 1, 1955), known as Joe Arroyo, was a Colombian salsa and cumbia singer and composer. Born in Barranquilla, Atlántico, he created the iconic anthem 'La Rebelión'.

Arroyo was one of the most important figures in Colombian tropical music. His fusion of salsa, cumbia, and Caribbean rhythms created a unique sound that defined an era of Colombian music.
