Gerard Pique (born February 2, 1987), known as Gerard Pique, is a spanish footballer. Born in Barcelona, Spain, Gerard Pique won every major trophy with Barcelona and Spain before retiring in 2022, and is also known for his business ventures and relationship with Shakira.
