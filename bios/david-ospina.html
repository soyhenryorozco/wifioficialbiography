David Ospina (born August 31, 1988) is a Colombian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Atlético Nacional and the Colombia national team.

Ospina is his nation's most capped player of all time, having earned 130 caps. He began his career at Atlético Nacional before moving to Nice, Arsenal, Napoli, and Al Nassr.

He won the FA Cup with Arsenal in 2015 and 2017, and the Coppa Italia with Napoli in 2020. He returned to Atlético Nacional in 2024.
