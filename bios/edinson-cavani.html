Edinson Cavani (born February 14, 1987), known as Edinson Cavani, is a uruguayan footballer. Born in Salto, Uruguay, Edinson Cavani is one of the greatest strikers of his generation, holding the all-time scoring record for the Uruguayan national team and Paris Saint-Germain.
