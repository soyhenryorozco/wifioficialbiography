Felipe Neto (born January 21, 1988), known as Felipe Neto, is a brazilian youtuber and entrepreneur. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Neto has over 45 million YouTube subscribers, making him one of Brazil's most-watched content creators. He is known for reaction videos and has launched several business ventures.
