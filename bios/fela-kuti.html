Biography

Fela Kuti (1938) is a nigerian singer.

Nigerian musician

Fela Kuti began their career in Nigerian and has achieved international recognition for their work as a singer. With a career spanning multiple decades, they have become one of the most recognized figures in their field.

Throughout their professional journey, Fela Kuti has received numerous accolades and recognition for their contributions to Singer. They continue to be an influential figure and role model for aspiring professionals worldwide.
