Biography

Gaudelia Díaz is a Singer.

Gaudelia Díaz Romero (born in 1964) is a Mexican former Paralympic athlete who competed mainly in B1 category distance events. She is also a singer, pianist, and actress who has performed under the pseudonym Crystal since the 1980s. Among her hits are "Suavemente" and "No Me Pregunten Por Él". In the 1990s, Díaz was part of the Mexican team that traveled to Barcelona, Spain for the 1992 Summer Paralympics. There she competed in the 800 meters (where she finished fourth), 1500m (where she finished fourth) and 3000m, in which she won a bronze medal.
