Tainá Müller (born June 1, 1982), known as Tainá Müller, is a brazilian actress. Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil, Tainá Müller is a Brazilian actress known for roles in Tropa de Elite and numerous telenovelas. She is one of Brazil's most popular actresses.
