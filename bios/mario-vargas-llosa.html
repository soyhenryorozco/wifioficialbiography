Mario Vargas Llosa (born March 28, 1936), known as Mario Vargas Llosa, is a peruvian-spanish writer and politician. Born in Arequipa, Peru, Nobel Prize-winning author of 'The Time of the Hero' and 'The Feast of the Goat'. He ran for president of Peru in 1990 and is one of Latin America's most celebrated writers.
