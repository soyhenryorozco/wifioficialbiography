Emerson Fittipaldi (born December 12, 1946), known as Emerson Fittipaldi, is a brazilian formula one driver. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, A two-time Formula One World Champion, Fittipaldi was the youngest F1 champion at age 25. He later won the Indianapolis 500 and became a successful team owner.
