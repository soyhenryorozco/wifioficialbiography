Usain Bolt (born August 21, 1986), known as Usain Bolt, is a jamaican sprinter. Born in Trelawny, Jamaica, Usain Bolt is the fastest human in history, holding world records in the 100m and 200m and winning eight Olympic gold medals across three Games.
