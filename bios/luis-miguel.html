Luis Miguel (born April 19, 1970), known as Luis Miguel, is a mexican singer. Born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, Luis Miguel is a Mexican singer known as El Sol de México. He is one of the most successful Latin artists in history with over 100 million records sold.
