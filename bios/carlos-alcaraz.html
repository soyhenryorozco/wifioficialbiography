Carlos Alcaraz (born May 5, 2003), known as Carlos Alcaraz, is a spanish tennis player. Born in El Palmar, Spain, Carlos Alcaraz became the youngest world number one in ATP history and won Wimbledon 2023 at age 20, hailed as the future of men's tennis.
