Sergio Henao (born December 10, 1987), known as Sergio Henao, is a colombian professional cyclist. Born in Rionegro, Colombia, A key domestique and stage race rider, Henao helped Chris Froome win multiple Tour de France titles. He won the Tour of California and the Vuelta a Colombia.
