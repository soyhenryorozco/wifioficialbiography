Leonardo Sbaraglia (born June 30, 1963), known as Leonardo Sbaraglia, is a argentine-spanish actor. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Leonardo Sbaraglia is an Argentine-Spanish actor known for Wild Tales and Intimate Stories. He has worked extensively in both Argentine and Spanish cinema.
