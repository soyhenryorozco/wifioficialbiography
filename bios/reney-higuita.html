José René Higuita Zapata (born August 27, 1966) is a Colombian former football goalkeeper. Born in Medellín, Antioquia, he is famous for inventing the scorpion kick save.

Higuita's acrobatic style revolutionized the goalkeeper position. He is an iconic figure in Colombian football history.
