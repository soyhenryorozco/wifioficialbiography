Jake Paul (born January 17, 1997), known as Jake Paul, is a american youtuber and professional boxer. Born in Cleveland, Ohio, USA, Jake Paul is a YouTuber turned professional boxer who gained fame on Vine and Disney Channel. He has headlined major pay-per-view boxing events.
