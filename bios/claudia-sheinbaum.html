Claudia Sheinbaum (born June 24, 1962), known as Claudia Sheinbaum, is a mexican politician and scientist. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Sheinbaum became Mexico's first female president in October 2024. A former mayor of Mexico City and climate scientist, she is AMLO's political successor.
