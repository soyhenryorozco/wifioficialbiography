Ronaldinho Gaúcho (born March 21, 1980), known as Ronaldinho Gaúcho, is a brazilian professional footballer. Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil, Known for his exceptional dribbling skills and creativity, Ronaldinho won two FIFA World Player of the Year awards. He helped Brazil win the 2002 World Cup.
