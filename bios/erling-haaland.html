Erling Haaland (born July 21, 2000), known as Erling Haaland, is a norwegian professional footballer. Born in Leeds, England, Haaland broke the Premier League single-season scoring record in his debut season with Manchester City. He is known for his incredible physicality and goal-scoring instinct.
