Frederick Blantford Bate

Frederick Blantford Bate

Frederick Blantford Bate (13 November, 1886 – December 24, 1970) was an American journalist and radio broadcaster for the NBC network in Europe in the 1930s. He was a representative for NBC in United Kingdom and Western Europe before and during World War II. He was the husband of Vera Bate Lombardi, the British socialite, and the father of Bridget Bate Tichenor, a surrealist artist.
