Fede Vigevani (born May 15, 1998), known as Fede Vigevani, is a argentine youtuber and content creator. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Vigevani is a popular Argentine YouTuber known for his challenges, experiments, and pranks. He has over 20 million subscribers and collaborates with major brands.
