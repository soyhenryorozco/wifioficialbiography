Carla Rebecchi Carla Rebecchi (born 7 September 1984) is an Argentine field hockey player who won the bronze medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing and the silver medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London with the Argentina national field hockey team. In 2010, she won the World Cup in Rosario, Argentina. Carla has also won six Champions Trophy tournaments, the 2014–2015 World League, three medals at the Pan American Games and two Pan American Cups.

Career

After Luciana Aymar retired and Macarena Rodriguez was left out of the national team following the 2015 Pan American Games, she was named the team's captain. In February 2017 she announced her retirement after 290 matches and 153 goals, and returned after becoming a mother on 3 September 2018.
