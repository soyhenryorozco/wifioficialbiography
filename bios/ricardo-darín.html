Ricardo Darín (born January 16, 1957), known as Ricardo Darín, is a argentine actor. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Ricardo Darín is an Argentine actor considered one of the greatest Latin American actors. He starred in The Secret in Their Eyes and The Chinese Room.
