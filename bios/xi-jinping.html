Xi Jinping (born June 15, 1953), known as Xi Jinping, is a chinese president of china. Born in Beijing, China, Xi Jinping is the General Secretary of the Communist Party of China and President of the People's Republic of China. He is considered the most powerful Chinese leader since Mao Zedong.
