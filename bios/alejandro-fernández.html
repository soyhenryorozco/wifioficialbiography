Alejandro Fernández (born April 24, 1971), known as Alejandro Fernández, is a mexican singer. Born in Guadalajara, Mexico, Alejandro Fernández is a Mexican singer known as El Potrillo. He is one of the best-selling Latin artists with a career spanning over three decades.
