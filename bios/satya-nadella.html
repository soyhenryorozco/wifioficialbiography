Satya Nadella (born August 19, 1967), known as Satya Nadella, is a indian-american ceo of microsoft. Born in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, Satya Nadella has served as CEO of Microsoft since 2014, focusing on cloud computing and AI. He has led Microsoft to become one of the most valuable companies in the world.
