Marília Mendonça (born July 22, 1995), known as Marília Mendonça, is a brazilian singer. Born in Fronteiras, Brazil, Marília Mendonça was a Brazilian sertaneja singer known as the Queen of Sertanejo. She tragically passed away in 2021 and is deeply mourned.
