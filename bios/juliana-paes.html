Juliana Paes (born March 26, 1979), known as Juliana Paes, is a brazilian actress. Born in Nilópolis, Brazil, Juliana Paes is a Brazilian actress and model known for telenovelas and films. She is one of Brazil's most popular and recognized actresses.
