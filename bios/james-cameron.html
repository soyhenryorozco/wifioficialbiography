James Cameron (born August 16, 1954), known as James Cameron, is a canadian film director and producer. Born in Kapuskasing, Ontario, Canada, James Cameron is a film director known for blockbusters like Titanic, Avatar, and Terminator 2. Avatar remains the highest-grossing film ever.
