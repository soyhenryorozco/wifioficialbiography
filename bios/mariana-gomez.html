Mariana Gómez

Colombian actress and model.

ActressColombiaModelTelevision

Mariana Gómez is a Colombian actress and model. She has appeared in various Colombian television productions and is known for her work in telenovelas and series.

Gómez has built a successful career in Colombian entertainment, gaining recognition for her versatile acting abilities and screen presence.
