Roberto Carlos (born April 10, 1973), known as Roberto Carlos, is a brazilian footballer. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Roberto Carlos is famous for his thunderous left foot and the greatest free kick in football history against France in 1997, winning the World Cup with Brazil in 2002.
