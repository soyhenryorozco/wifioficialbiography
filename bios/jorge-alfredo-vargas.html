Jorge Alfredo Vargas (born June 12, 1965), known as Jorge Alfredo Vargas, is a colombian news anchor and journalist. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Jorge Alfredo Vargas is one of Colombia's most recognized news anchors, known for hosting Noticiero Caracol. He has decades of experience in broadcast journalism.
