Natalia Lafourcade (born February 26, 1984), known as Natalia Lafourcade, is a mexican singer. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Natalia Lafourcade is a Mexican singer known for blending pop, rock, and traditional Mexican music. She has won multiple Grammy and Latin Grammy Awards.
