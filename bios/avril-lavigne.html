Avril Lavigne (born September 27, 1984), known as Avril Lavigne, is a canadian singer and songwriter. Born in Belleville, Ontario, Canada, Avril Lavigne was the pop-punk queen of the 2000s with hits like Complicated and Sk8er Boi.
