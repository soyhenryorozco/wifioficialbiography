Gordon Ramsay (born November 8, 1966), known as Gordon Ramsay, is a british chef and television personality. Born in Johnstone, Scotland, UK, Gordon Ramsay is a Michelin-starred chef, restaurateur, and television personality known for shows like Hell's Kitchen and MasterChef.
