Camila Pitanga (born June 14, 1977), known as Camila Pitanga, is a brazilian actress. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Camila Pitanga is a Brazilian actress known for City of God and numerous telenovelas. She has become one of Brazil's most prominent actresses.
