Joe Frazier (born January 12, 1944), known as Joe Frazier, is a american boxer. Born in Beaufort, South Carolina, USA, Joe Frazier, known as Smokin' Joe, was the undisputed heavyweight champion and Muhammad Ali's greatest rival, winning the legendary Thrilla in Manila bout.
