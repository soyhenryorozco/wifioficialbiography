Pedri (born November 25, 2002), known as Pedri, is a spanish footballer. Born in Tegueste, Spain, Pedri has been hailed as the heir to Andres Iniesta's throne at Barcelona, winning the Golden Boy award and establishing himself as one of Europe's best midfielders.
