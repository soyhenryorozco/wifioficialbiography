Javier Mascherano (born June 8, 1984), known as Javier Mascherano, is a argentine footballer. Born in San Lorenzo, Argentina, Javier Mascherano was a defensive stalwart for Barcelona and Argentina, forming part of one of the greatest defensive midfields and backlines of his generation.
