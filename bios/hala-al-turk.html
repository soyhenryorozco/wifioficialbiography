Hala Al Turk

حلا موفق محمد الترك

Hala Al Turk is a Bahraini actress and singer. She became known for being a contestant on Arabs Got Talent in 2011. Al Turk then released many singles, including "Bnayty El Habooba (2011)" featuring singer Mashael, for which she become a popular child singer. In early 2015, she moved to her father's company, Al Turk Productions.
