Isabel Perón (born February 4, 1930), known as Isabel Perón, is a argentine politician. Born in La Rioja, Argentina, Isabel Perón became the world's first female president in 1974 after the death of her husband Juan Perón. Her presidency ended in a military coup in 1976.
