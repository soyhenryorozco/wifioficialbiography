Sebastián Yatra (born October 15, 1994), known as Sebastián Yatra, is a colombian singer. Born in Medellín, Colombia, Sebastián Yatra is a Colombian pop singer known for romantic ballads like Robarte un Beso and Tacones Rojos. He has won Latin Grammy Awards.
