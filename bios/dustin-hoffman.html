Dustin Hoffman (born August 8, 1937), known as Dustin Hoffman, is a american actor. Born in Los Angeles, California, USA, Dustin Hoffman is a two-time Academy Award-winning actor known for Rain Man, Kramer vs. Kramer, and The Graduate.
