Biography

Adriana Vargas is a Public Figure.

Adriana Vargas (born in Bogotá) is a Colombian journalist and news presenter for New York's WXTV Univision 41 Nueva York. Vargas majored in Social Communication from Pontifical Xavierian University in 1998, but she debuted in journalism two years before with a small production company. Later she presented several cultural shows for state-run Señal Colombia, and joined Manuel Teodoro to co-host the Caracol TV newsmagazine Séptimo día.
