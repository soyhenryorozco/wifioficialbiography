Iker Casillas (born May 20, 1981), known as Iker Casillas, is a spanish professional footballer. Born in Móstoles, Spain, Considered one of the greatest goalkeepers ever, Casillas won the 2010 World Cup and two European Championships with Spain. He spent most of his career at Real Madrid.
