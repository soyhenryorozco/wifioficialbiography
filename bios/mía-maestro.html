Mía Maestro (born June 19, 1978), known as Mía Maestro, is a argentine actress. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Mía Maestro is an Argentine actress and musician known for Narcos, Twilight, and The Motorcycle Diaries. She has also released music albums.
