Ricky Martin (born December 24, 1971), known as Ricky Martin, is a puerto rican singer. Born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, Ricky Martin is a Puerto Rican pop singer and actor who became a global icon with hits like Livin la Vida Loca. He has sold over 70 million records.
