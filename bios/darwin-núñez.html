Darwin Núñez (born June 24, 1999), known as Darwin Núñez, is a uruguayan professional footballer. Born in Artigas, Uruguay, Núñez moved from Benfica to Liverpool for a British record fee. He is known for his explosive pace and clinical finishing as a central striker.
