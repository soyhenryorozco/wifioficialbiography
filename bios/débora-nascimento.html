Débora Nascimento (born June 16, 1985), known as Débora Nascimento, is a brazilian actress. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Débora Nascimento is a Brazilian actress and model known for roles in Elite Squad and Aloysio. She is one of Brazil's rising young actresses.
