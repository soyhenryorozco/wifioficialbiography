Hirving Lozano (born July 30, 1995), known as Hirving Lozano, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Known as 'Chucky', Lozano scored the winning goal against Germany at the 2018 World Cup. He played for PSV and Napoli before joining San Diego FC.
