Susan Wojcicki (born July 5, 1968), known as Susan Wojcicki, is a american former ceo of youtube. Born in Santa Clara, California, USA, Susan Wojcicki served as CEO of YouTube from 2014 to 2023. She was Google's first marketing manager and played a key role in the acquisition of YouTube.
