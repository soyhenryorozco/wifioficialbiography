Kobe Bryant (born August 23, 1978), known as Kobe Bryant, is a american basketball player. Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA, Kobe Bryant won five NBA championships with the Los Angeles Lakers and was known for his Mamba Mentality, tragically passing away in a helicopter crash in 2020.
