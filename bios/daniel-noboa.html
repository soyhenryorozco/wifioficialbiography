Daniel Noboa (born June 30, 1987), known as Daniel Noboa, is a ecuadorian politician and businessman. Born in Guayaquil, Ecuador, At 35, Noboa became Ecuador's youngest president in 2023. He has focused on addressing the country's security crisis and attracting foreign investment.
