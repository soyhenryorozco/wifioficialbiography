Dennis Bergkamp (born May 10, 1969), known as Dennis Bergkamp, is a dutch footballer. Born in Amsterdam, Netherlands, Dennis Bergkamp is an Arsenal legend celebrated for his sublime technical ability and iconic goals, including his famous strike against Argentina in the 1998 World Cup.
