MC Kevinho (born September 24, 1993), known as MC Kevinho, is a brazilian singer. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, MC Kevinho is a Brazilian singer and rapper known for funk hits and viral music videos. He has millions of followers and is one of Brazil's most-streamed artists.
