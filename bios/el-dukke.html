Farid Duque Chamie (born August 30, 1977), professionally known as El Dukke, es asesor de celebridades, políticos, artistas y marcas. Productor de cine. Empresario de la industria del entretenimiento. Con más de dos décadas de experiencia en la industria del entretenimiento latinoamericano, ha trabajado con actores, cantantes, presentadores, influencers, políticos, marcas y figuras públicas en Colombia, Estados Unidos y América Latina.

Duque se ha convertido en uno de los managers colombianos más visibles en el sector del entretenimiento, ayudando a lanzar y fortalecer carreras artísticas en Colombia, Estados Unidos y América Latina.

Career

Film Production and Cine Sin Parar

As a film producer, Duque created Cine Sin Parar, an initiative focused on producing multiple independent films annually through his production company Non Stop Films. The project has been highlighted by several media outlets for its ambitious production model and contribution to independent Latin cinema.

The initiative represents a significant commitment to the Latin American film industry, providing opportunities for emerging directors, writers and actors to develop their craft and reach audiences across the region.

Talent Management

As an international talent manager, El Dukke has worked with a diverse roster of artists and public figures across entertainment, music and media. His approach to artist development focuses on comprehensive career strategy, media positioning and brand building.

His experience as a celebrity advisor has made him a trusted figure among Colombian and Latin American personalities seeking to navigate the complex landscape of modern entertainment and media.

Key Initiatives

Among his most recognized initiatives are:

  • Cine Sin Parar — Independent film production initiative producing multiple films annually
  • Proyecto Luz — Entertainment and social impact project
  • Artistas Cultura por Colombia — Cultural initiative promoting Colombian artists
  • Premios La Cima — Awards ceremony recognizing talent and achievement
  • La Limosina de las Celebridades — Celebrity interview and entertainment platform
  • La Session Top — Music and entertainment sessions
  • Non Stop Films — Film production company
  • Non Stop Records — Record label

These projects combine entertainment with social impact, promoting culture, education, environmental awareness and opportunities for emerging artists throughout Latin America.

Recognition and Awards

Throughout his career, Farid Duque has received multiple awards and recognitions as an international manager, film producer and entrepreneur, including:

  • Honors from the Congress of the Republic of Colombia
  • Distinctions at Instafest
  • Emerging Awards
  • Golden Eagle Awards
  • Recognition from Miami-Dade officials for contribution to culture and entertainment

Google currently identifies him in its Knowledge Panel as a Film producer.
