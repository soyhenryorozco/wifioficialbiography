Mon Laferte (born May 2, 1983), known as Mon Laferte, is a chilean-mexican singer. Born in Viña del Mar, Chile, Mon Laferte is a Chilean-Mexican singer known for her powerful voice and politically charged lyrics. She has won multiple Latin Grammy Awards.
