Sundar Pichai (born June 10, 1972), known as Sundar Pichai, is a indian-american ceo of alphabet and google. Born in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India, Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and Google LLC. He played a key role in developing Google Chrome and Chrome OS.
