Lamine Yamal (born July 13, 2007), known as Lamine Yamal, is a spanish footballer. Born in Esplugues de Llobregat, Spain, Lamine Yamal became the youngest player to appear and score at a European Championship in 2024, emerging as the most exciting teenage talent in world football.
