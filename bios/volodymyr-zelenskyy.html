Volodymyr Zelenskyy (born January 25, 1978), known as Volodymyr Zelenskyy, is a ukrainian president of ukraine. Born in Kryvyi Rih, Ukrainian SSR, Soviet Union, Volodymyr Zelenskyy is the President of Ukraine, in office since 2019. A former comedian and actor, he gained worldwide recognition for his leadership during the Russian invasion.
