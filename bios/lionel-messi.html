Lionel Messi (born June 24, 1987), known as Lionel Messi, is a argentine professional footballer. Born in Rosario, Argentina, Regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, Messi has won a record eight Ballon d'Or awards. He led Argentina to victory in the 2022 FIFA World Cup.
