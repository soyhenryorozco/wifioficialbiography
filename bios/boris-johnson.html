Boris Johnson (born June 19, 1964), known as Boris Johnson, is a british former prime minister of the united kingdom. Born in New York City, New York, USA, Boris Johnson served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 2019 to 2022. He previously served as Mayor of London and was a leading figure in the Brexit campaign.
