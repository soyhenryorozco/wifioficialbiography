Taliana María Vargas Carrillo (born December 20, 1987) is a Colombian actress, model, and beauty queen. Born in Sincelejo, Sucre, she was crowned Miss Colombia 2007 and was second runner-up at Miss Universe 2008.

After her pageant career, Vargas became a successful actress in Colombian television and film.
