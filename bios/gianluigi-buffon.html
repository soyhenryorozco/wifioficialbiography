Gianluigi Buffon (born January 28, 1978), known as Gianluigi Buffon, is a italian footballer. Born in Carrara, Italy, Gianluigi Buffon is one of the greatest goalkeepers ever, holding the record for most Serie A appearances and winning the 2006 World Cup with Italy.
