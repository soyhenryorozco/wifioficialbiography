Oribe Peralta (born January 12, 1984), known as Oribe Peralta, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Torreón, Mexico, Peralta scored the opening goal in the 2012 Olympic gold medal match for Mexico. He was the top scorer in Liga MX for multiple seasons with Santos Laguna.
