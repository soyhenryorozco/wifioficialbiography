Samuel Eto'o (born March 10, 1981), known as Samuel Eto'o, is a cameroonian footballer. Born in Douala, Cameroon, Samuel Eto'o is a four-time African Player of the Year who starred for Barcelona and Inter Milan, winning the treble with both clubs.
