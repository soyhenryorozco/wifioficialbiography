Dwyane Wade (born January 17, 1982), known as Dwyane Wade, is a american basketball player. Born in Chicago, Illinois, USA, Dwyane Wade won three NBA championships with the Miami Heat and was named the 2006 Finals MVP, known for his explosive athleticism and clutch performances.
