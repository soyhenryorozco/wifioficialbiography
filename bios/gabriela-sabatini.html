Gabriela Sabatini (born May 16, 1970), known as Gabriela Sabatini, is a argentine professional tennis player. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Sabatini won the 1990 US Open and was ranked as high as world number three. She was one of the most popular athletes in Latin America during the 1990s.
