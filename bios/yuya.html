Yuya (born March 7, 1993), known as Yuya, is a mexican youtuber and influencer. Born in Monterrey, Mexico, Yuya is one of the most followed Spanish-speaking YouTubers, with over 30 million subscribers. She creates lifestyle, beauty, and comedy content and has published bestselling books.
