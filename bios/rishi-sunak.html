Rishi Sunak (born May 12, 1980), known as Rishi Sunak, is a british prime minister of the united kingdom. Born in Southampton, England, UK, Rishi Sunak became Prime Minister of the United Kingdom in October 2022. He previously served as Chancellor of the Exchequer under Boris Johnson.
