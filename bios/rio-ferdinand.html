Rio Ferdinand (born November 7, 1978), known as Rio Ferdinand, is a english footballer and pundit. Born in London, England, Rio Ferdinand was one of the most elegant center-backs in Premier League history, forming a formidable partnership with Nemanja Vidic at Manchester United.
