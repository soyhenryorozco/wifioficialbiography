Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie (born September 15, 1977), known as Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie, is a nigerian author. Born in Enugu, Nigeria, Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie is the author of Half of a Yellow Sun and Americanah, and her TED talk The Danger of a Single Story has been viewed over 30 million times.
