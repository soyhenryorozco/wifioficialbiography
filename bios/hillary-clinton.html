Hillary Clinton (born October 26, 1947), known as Hillary Clinton, is a american former u.s. secretary of state. Born in Chicago, Illinois, USA, Hillary Clinton served as U.S. Secretary of State from 2009 to 2013 and was the Democratic nominee for President in 2016. She also served as a U.S. Senator from New York.
