Antonio Cervantes Resende (born December 15, 1945), known as Kid Pambele, is a Colombian boxer. Born on Isla de San Bernardo, Bolívar, he was a two-time world champion in the junior welterweight division.

Cervantes held the WBA junior welterweight title from 1972 to 1976 and again from 1977 to 1978. He is considered one of the greatest Colombian boxers in history.
