Juanes (born August 9, 1972) is a Colombian musician and singer. Since releasing his solo debut album Fíjate Bien in 2000, Juanes has won 26 Latin Grammy Awards and sold more than 15 million records worldwide, making him one of the best-selling Latin music artists of all time.

Born in Medellín, Colombia, Juanes was a member of the rock band Ekhymosis before launching his solo career. His music blends Latin rock, rock en español, Latin pop, and cumbia.
