Daniel Felipe Martínez (born April 25, 1997), known as Daniel Felipe Martínez, is a colombian professional cyclist. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Martínez won the Tour de Suisse and has been a strong stage race contender. He rode for Ineos Grenadiers before joining BORA-Hansgrohe.
