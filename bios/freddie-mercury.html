Freddie Mercury (born September 5, 1946), known as Freddie Mercury, is a british-zanzibari singer and songwriter. Born in Stone Town, Zanzibar, Freddie Mercury was the legendary frontman of Queen, known for his theatrical stage presence and four-octave vocal range.
