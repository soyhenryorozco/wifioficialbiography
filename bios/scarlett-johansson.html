Scarlett Johansson (born November 22, 1984), known as Scarlett Johansson, is a american actress. Born in New York City, New York, USA, Scarlett Johansson is one of the highest-grossing actresses of all time, known for her role as Black Widow in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
