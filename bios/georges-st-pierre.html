Georges St-Pierre (born May 19, 1981), known as Georges St-Pierre, is a canadian mma fighter. Born in Saint-Isidore, Canada, Georges St-Pierre is considered the greatest welterweight in MMA history, holding the title for nearly five years and returning to win the middleweight championship.
