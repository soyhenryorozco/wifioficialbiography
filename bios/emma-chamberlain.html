Emma Chamberlain (born May 22, 2001), known as Emma Chamberlain, is a american youtuber and entrepreneur. Born in San Bruno, California, USA, Emma Chamberlain is a YouTuber known for her innovative editing style and relatable content. She founded Chamberlain Coffee and has collaborated with major fashion brands.
