Juan Cuadrado (born May 26, 1988) is a Colombian professional footballer who usually plays on the right side of the pitch, either as a winger, wing-back, or full-back.

Cuadrado is known for his direct style of play, including his pace both on and off the ball, as well as his dribbling skills. He has earned over 110 caps for Colombia.

His club career includes spells at Udinese, Fiorentina, Chelsea, Juventus, Inter Milan, Atalanta, and Pisa. He was featured on the Latin American cover of FIFA 16.
