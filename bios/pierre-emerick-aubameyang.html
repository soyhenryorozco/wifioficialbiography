Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang (born June 18, 1989), known as Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang, is a gabonese footballer. Born in Laval, France, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang is a prolific striker who has played for Borussia Dortmund, Arsenal, and Barcelona, and served as captain of the Gabonese national team.
