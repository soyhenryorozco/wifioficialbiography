Juan Martín del Potro (born September 23, 1988), known as Juan Martín del Potro, is a argentine professional tennis player. Born in Tandil, Argentina, Del Potro won the 2009 US Open and an Olympic silver medal in 2012. Known for his powerful forehand, he inspired Argentina to a Davis Cup victory in 2016.
