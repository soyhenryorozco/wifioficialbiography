Silvestre Dangond Corredor (born May 2, 1980) is a Colombian vallenato singer and television personality. Born in Valledupar, Cesar, he is one of the most popular vallenato artists of his generation.

Dangond has released numerous successful albums and has been a judge on Colombian television music shows. He blends traditional vallenato with modern pop influences.
