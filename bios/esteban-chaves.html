Esteban Chaves (born July 17, 1990), known as Esteban Chaves, is a colombian professional cyclist. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Chaves became the first Colombian to win a stage in the Giro d'Italia. He finished second in the 2016 Giro and has been a consistent Grand Tour contender.
