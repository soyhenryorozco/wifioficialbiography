Biography

Al Barr is a Singer.

Alexander Martin Barr (born January 21, 1968) is an American musician. He is best known for his time as the lead singer of the Dropkick Murphys, whom he joined in 1998. As of 2022, he is on hiatus from the band to care for his mother. He was also a founder and lead singer for The Bruisers, which he helped form in 1988 in Portsmouth, New Hampshire.
