Toño Rosario (born August 27, 1955), known as Toño Rosario, is a dominican singer. Born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, Toño Rosario is a Dominican singer and percussionist known as a merengue legend. He was a founding member of Los Hermanos Rosario.
