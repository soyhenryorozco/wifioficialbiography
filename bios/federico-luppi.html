Federico Luppi (born February 23, 1936), known as Federico Luppi, is a argentine-spanish actor. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Federico Luppi was an Argentine-Spanish actor known for his work with director Álex de la Iglesia and in Pan's Labyrinth. He passed away in 2017.
