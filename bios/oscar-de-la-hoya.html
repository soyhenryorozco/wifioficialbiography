Oscar De La Hoya (born February 4, 1973), known as Oscar De La Hoya, is a american professional boxer and promoter. Born in East Los Angeles, United States, The 'Golden Boy' won Olympic gold in 1992 and world titles in six weight divisions. He founded Golden Boy Promotions and became one of boxing's most influential figures.
