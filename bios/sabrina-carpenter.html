Sabrina Carpenter (born May 11, 1999), known as Sabrina Carpenter, is a american singer and actress. Born in Lehigh Valley, Pennsylvania, USA, Sabrina Carpenter transitioned from Disney to pop stardom with hits like Espresso and Please Please Please.
