Olaf Scholz (born June 14, 1958), known as Olaf Scholz, is a german chancellor of germany. Born in Osnabrueck, West Germany, Olaf Scholz has served as Chancellor of Germany since 2021. He previously served as Vice Chancellor and Minister of Finance under Angela Merkel.
