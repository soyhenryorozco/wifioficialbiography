Andrés Iniesta (born May 11, 1984), known as Andrés Iniesta, is a spanish professional footballer. Born in Fuentealbilla, Spain, Iniesta scored the winning goal in the 2010 World Cup final for Spain. He is celebrated for his elegant dribbling and vision as a midfielder at Barcelona.
