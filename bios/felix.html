Felix Yongbok Lee (born September 15, 2000), known mononymously as Felix (Korean: 필릭스), is an Australian rapper, dancer and singer based in South Korea. He is a member of the South Korean boy band Stray Kids, formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017. He officially debuted with the group on March 25, 2018.

Felix is widely recognized for his unusually deep voice which contrasts with his youthful appearance, his distinctive freckles, and his exceptional dance performance skills. He is one of the most popular Australian K-pop idols internationally.

Early life and education

Felix was born on September 15, 2000, in Sydney, Australia to South Korean parents. He grew up in the Sydney area and attended St Patrick's Marist College in Dundas, New South Wales. He has an older sister named Rachel. He developed an interest in K-pop and dance at a young age.

In 2016, while attending a K-pop concert in Sydney, Felix was scouted by a talent agent from JYP Entertainment. He successfully auditioned and moved to Seoul, South Korea to begin his training as a K-pop idol. He trained for approximately two years, focusing on dance, rap, Korean language, and performance.

Career

Pre-debut and training

Felix was introduced as a member of the pre-debut group Stray Kids through the reality show "Stray Kids" (2017) on Mnet. The show documented the training and evaluation process of the members. Despite initial elimination concerns, he was ultimately confirmed as a final member of the group.

Debut with Stray Kids

Stray Kids officially debuted on March 25, 2018, with the extended play I Am Not and the title track "District 9". Felix contributed to the group's performance and choreography from the beginning, showcasing his dance expertise. The group quickly gained a dedicated fanbase known as STAY.

Since debut, Stray Kids have released multiple successful albums including I Am Who (2018), I Am You (2018), Clé Series (2019), GO LIVE (2020), NOEASY (2021), MAXIDENT (2022), 5-STAR (2023), and ROCK-STAR (2023).

Rise to international fame

Stray Kids achieved their first music show win in 2019 and have since become one of the best-selling K-pop groups globally. Their album 5-STAR debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, and MAXIDENT also topped the chart. The group has sold millions of albums worldwide and has headlined international tours.

Felix has gained significant individual recognition. His deep voice has gone viral multiple times on social media platforms like TikTok and Twitter. He has been featured in fashion magazines and has become a style icon. He was appointed as a model ambassador for several international brands.

Artistry

Felix is known for his deep bass voice, which is rare in K-pop and has become his signature trait. He often delivers the "killing parts" in Stray Kids' songs with his impactful rap verses. His dance style combines sharp, precise movements with fluidity, and he frequently participates in the group's performance direction.

He has cited Chris Brown and BTS as musical influences. He is also influenced by Australian artists and brings a unique international perspective to the group's music.

Achievements

  • Stray Kids' album 5-STAR (2023) debuted at #1 on Billboard 200
  • MAXIDENT (2022) also reached #1 on Billboard 200

  • Golden Disc Awards winners
  • Seoul Music Awards winners

  • Recognized as one of the most influential K-pop idols globally
  • Fashion ambassador for luxury brands

Personal life

Felix is known for his warm and cheerful personality. He is fluent in both Korean and English and frequently serves as a translator for his group members. He has a close friendship with his fellow Stray Kids members, particularly Bang Chan, who is also Australian-Korean.

He has been open about his Christian faith and values. He enjoys gaming, photography, and spending time with his members. He is known for his love of food and often shares cooking content with fans.
