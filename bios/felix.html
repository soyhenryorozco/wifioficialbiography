Felix

Felix Lee · Lee Felix · Stray Kids Dancer, Rapper

Australian dancer, rapper of Stray Kids, known for deep voice and dance skills

Felix (born September 15, 2000), also known as Felix Lee or Lee Felix, is an Australian dancer, rapper and singer. He is a member of Stray Kids, one of the most successful fourth-generation K-pop groups.

Known for his deep voice, exceptional dance skills, and freckles, Felix is one of the most recognized Australian idols in K-pop.

Early Life

Felix was born in Sydney, Australia to Korean parents. He studied at St Patrick's Marist College before moving to South Korea to pursue training under JYP Entertainment.

Career

Felix debuted with Stray Kids in March 2018. The group has achieved massive international success with albums like GO LIVE, NOEASY, and 5-STAR.
