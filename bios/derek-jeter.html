Derek Jeter (born June 26, 1974), known as Derek Jeter, is a american baseball player and executive. Born in Pequannock, New Jersey, USA, Derek Jeter won five World Series championships with the New York Yankees and was known as The Captain, later becoming CEO of the Miami Marlins.
