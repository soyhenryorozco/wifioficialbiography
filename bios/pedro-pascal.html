Jose Pedro Balmaceda Pascal (born April 2, 1975), known as Pedro Pascal, is a Chilean-American actor. Born in Santiago, Chile, he is known for his roles in Game of Thrones, Narcos, The Mandalorian, and The Last of Us.

Pascal gained widespread recognition for his portrayal of Oberyn Martell in Game of Thrones (2014) and as Javier Pena in Narcos (2015-2017). He became a global star as the title character in The Mandalorian (2019-2023) and as Joel Miller in The Last of Us (2023-2025).

In film, Pascal has starred in blockbusters such as Wonder Woman 1984 (2020), Gladiator II (2024), and The Fantastic Four: First Steps (2025).
