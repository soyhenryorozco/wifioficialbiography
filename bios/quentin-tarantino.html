Quentin Tarantino (born March 27, 1963), known as Quentin Tarantino, is a american film director and screenwriter. Born in Knoxville, Tennessee, USA, Quentin Tarantino is an acclaimed film director known for his distinctive style in films like Pulp Fiction, Kill Bill, and Inglourious Basterds.
