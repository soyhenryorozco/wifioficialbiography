David Beckham (born May 2, 1975), known as David Beckham, is a english professional footballer. Born in London, England, One of the most famous footballers in history, Beckham is known for his exceptional free kicks and crossing ability. He played for Manchester United, Real Madrid, and LA Galaxy.
