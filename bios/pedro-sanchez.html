Pedro Sanchez (born February 29, 1972), known as Pedro Sanchez, is a spanish prime minister of spain. Born in Madrid, Spain, Pedro Sanchez has served as Prime Minister of Spain since 2018. He is the leader of the Spanish Socialist Workers Party.
