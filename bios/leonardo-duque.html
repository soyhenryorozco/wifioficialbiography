Leonardo Duque Leonardo Fabio Duque (born April 10, 1980) is a French-Colombian professional road racing cyclist, who last rode for the Delko team. After stage 19 of the 2009 Tour de France, Duque was named the most combative rider of the stage after aggressively pacing a breakaway. In 2011 he became the first Colombian-born cyclist to finish the cobbled One Day Cycling Monuments, the Tour of Flanders and Paris–Roubaix. He also competed in the men's Madison at the 2004 Summer Olympics.
