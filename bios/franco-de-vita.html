Franco De Vita (born January 23, 1954), known as Franco De Vita, is a venezuelan singer. Born in Caracas, Venezuela, Franco De Vita is a Venezuelan pop singer known for socially conscious lyrics. He has won multiple Latin Grammy Awards.
