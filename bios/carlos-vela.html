Carlos Vela (born March 1, 1989), known as Carlos Vela, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Cancún, Mexico, Vela was named MLS MVP in 2019 after a record-breaking season with LAFC. He previously played for Arsenal and Real Sociedad in Europe.
