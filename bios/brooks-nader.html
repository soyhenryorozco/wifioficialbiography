Brooks Nader

Brooks Nader

Brooks Claire Nader is an American model and television personality. She became a model for Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue after winning the 2019 Swim Search, and was featured in the magazine in 2020, 2021, and 2022. In 2023, Nader became a covergirl for the magazine. In 2024, she competed on the 33rd season of Dancing with the Stars. She stars along with her sisters in their reality show Love Thy Nader and Fox's upcoming 2027 reboot of Baywatch.
