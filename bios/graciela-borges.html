Graciela Borges (born February 26, 1941), known as Graciela Borges, is a argentine actress. Born in Lomas de Zamora, Argentina, Graciela Borges is an Argentine actress with a career spanning over six decades. She is one of the greatest actresses in Argentine cinema history.
