Mónica Jaramillo (born September 8, 1983), known as Mónica Jaramillo, is a colombian journalist and news anchor. Born in Medellín, Colombia, Mónica Jaramillo is a Colombian journalist and news anchor known for her work on Noticias Caracol. She is recognized for her professional and empathetic reporting style.
