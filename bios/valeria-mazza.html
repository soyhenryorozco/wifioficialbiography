Alejandra María Mazza (born February 17, 1972), known as Valeria Mazza, is an Argentine supermodel and businesswoman. She is one of the most famous Latin American models of all time.

Mazza appeared on the cover of Sports Illustrated and has been the face of major international fashion brands. She is also a successful entrepreneur and philanthropist.
