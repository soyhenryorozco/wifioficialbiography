Luis Scola (born April 30, 1980), known as Luis Scola, is a argentine professional basketball player. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Scola had a 10-year NBA career and is Argentina's all-time leading scorer in international basketball. He won Olympic gold in 2004 alongside Ginóbili.
