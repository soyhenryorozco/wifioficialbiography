Rommel Pacheco Rommel Agmed Pacheco Marrufo (born 12 July 1986) is a Mexican politician and retired diver. He was a six-time Pan American Games medalist (three gold, three silver). In the 2004 Summer Olympics he finished in 10th place in the 10-meter platform and 3-meter springboard. In the 2008 Summer Olympics he finished in 8th place in the 10-meter platform.

Career

In the 2021 mid-terms Pacheco was elected to the Chamber of Deputies to represent the third district of Yucatán for the National Action Party (PAN). In October 2024, he was appointed by President Claudia Sheinbaum as the new head of the National Commission for Physical Culture and Sport (CONADE), replacing Ana Gabriela Guevara.
