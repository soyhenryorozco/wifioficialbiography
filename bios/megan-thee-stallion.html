Megan Thee Stallion (born February 15, 1995), known as Megan Thee Stallion, is a american rapper and singer. Born in Houston, Texas, USA, Megan Thee Stallion is a Grammy-winning rapper known for her confident style and hits like Savage and Hot Girl Summer.
