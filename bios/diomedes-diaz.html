Diomedes Díaz (born May 26, 1957), known as Diomedes Díaz, is a colombian singer. Born in La Jagua de Ibirico, Colombia, Diomedes Díaz was a Colombian vallenato singer known as El Cacique. He is considered one of the greatest vallenato artists of all time.
