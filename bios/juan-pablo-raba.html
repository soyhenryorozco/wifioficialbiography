Juan Pablo Raba Vidal (born January 14, 1977) is a Colombian actor, producer, and presenter. Born in Bogota, he is one of the most recognized faces in Colombian and Latin American television.

Raba gained international recognition for his role as Gustavo Gaviria in the Netflix series Narcos (2015). In Colombia, he is known for starring in popular telenovelas like Rosario Tijeras, Secretos de familia, and El robo del siglo.

He has also appeared in Hollywood productions including The 33 (2015) and the Netflix film 6 Underground (2019) alongside Ryan Reynolds.
