Ana Lucía Domínguez (born December 2, 1978), known as Ana Lucía Domínguez, is a colombian actress. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Colombian actress known for her roles in telenovelas like Rosario Tijeras. She has been a prominent figure in Colombian television.
