Ícaro Silva (born September 20, 1975), known as Ícaro Silva, is a brazilian actor. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Ícaro Silva is a Brazilian actor known for roles in Elite Squad and other acclaimed Brazilian productions. He is known for intense dramatic performances.
