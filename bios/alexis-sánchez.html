Alexis Sánchez (born December 12, 1988), known as Alexis Sánchez, is a chilean professional footballer. Born in Tocopilla, Chile, Chile's all-time leading scorer, Sánchez won two Copa América titles. He starred for Barcelona, Arsenal, and Inter Milan with his pace and technical skill.
