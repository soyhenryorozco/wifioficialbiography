Justin Trudeau (born December 25, 1971), known as Justin Trudeau, is a canadian prime minister of canada. Born in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, Justin Trudeau has served as Prime Minister of Canada since 2015, leading the Liberal Party. He is the son of former Prime Minister Pierre Trudeau.
