Carl Lewis (born July 1, 1961), known as Carl Lewis, is a american sprinter and long jumper. Born in Birmingham, Alabama, USA, Carl Lewis won nine Olympic gold medals across sprints and long jump, dominating track and field for over a decade and being named Athlete of the Century.
