Billie Joe Armstrong (born February 17, 1972), known as Billie Joe Armstrong, is a american singer and guitarist. Born in Oakland, United States, Billie Joe Armstrong is the lead vocalist of Green Day, who helped popularize punk rock in the mainstream.
