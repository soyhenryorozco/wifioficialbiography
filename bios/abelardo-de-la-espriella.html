Abelardo de la Espriella

President-elect of Colombia · Defensores de la Patria

President-elect of Colombia, lawyer, businessman and politician. Founder of the Defensores de la Patria movement. Will assume office on August 7, 2026.

Politics Colombia President Defensores de la Patria

Abelardo Gabriel de la Espriella Otero (born July 31, 1978, in Bogotá, Colombia) is a Colombian lawyer, businessman and politician. He is the president-elect of Colombia, set to assume office on August 7, 2026.

De la Espriella founded the political movement Defensores de la Patria in 2024. In 2026, he ran as a candidate for the Presidency of Colombia through a Significant Group of Citizens (G.S.C.), using the same name as his movement.

He is considered a right-wing populist. His presidential campaign focuses on promoting a "tough on crime" policy, the defense of "the traditional family" and the "defense of the homeland." In economic matters, he defends free market policies, reduction of the size of the state, decreased public spending, and protection of private property.

As a lawyer and businessman, De la Espriella brings a private sector perspective to Colombian politics. His election marks a significant shift in the country's political landscape.
