Jennifer Lawrence (born August 15, 1990), known as Jennifer Lawrence, is a american actress. Born in Indian Hills, Kentucky, USA, Jennifer Lawrence is an Oscar-winning actress known for The Hunger Games series and Silver Linings Playbook.
