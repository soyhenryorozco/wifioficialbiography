Travis Kalanick (born August 6, 1976), known as Travis Kalanick, is a american co-founder of uber. Born in Los Angeles, California, USA, Travis Kalanick co-founded Uber and served as its CEO from 2010 to 2017. He also co-founded the cloud kitchen company CloudKitchens.
