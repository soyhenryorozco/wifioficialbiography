El Alfa (born December 18, 1990), known as El Alfa, is a dominican singer. Born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, El Alfa is a Dominican dembow artist known as El Jefe. He has become the biggest name in Dominican dembow and collaborated with Cardi B and Bad Bunny.
