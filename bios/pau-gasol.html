Pau Gasol (born July 6, 1980), known as Pau Gasol, is a spanish professional basketball player. Born in Barcelona, Spain, A two-time NBA champion with the Lakers, Gasol is considered one of the greatest European basketball players. He won EuroBasket MVP twice and an Olympic silver medal.
