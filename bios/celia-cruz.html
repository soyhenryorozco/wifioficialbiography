Celia Cruz (born October 21, 1925), known as Celia Cruz, is a cuban singer. Born in Havana, Cuba, Celia Cruz was a Cuban singer known as the Queen of Salsa. She had one of the most influential careers in Latin music history and won multiple Grammy Awards.
