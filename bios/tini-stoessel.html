Tini Stoessel (born June 21, 1997), known as Tini Stoessel, is a argentine singer. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Tini Stoessel is an Argentine singer and actress who gained fame through Disney Channel's Violetta. She has become a major Latin pop star.
