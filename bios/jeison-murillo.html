Jeison Murillo (born May 27, 1992), known as Jeison Murillo, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Cali, Colombia, Murillo won the Coppa Italia with Inter Milan and has played for Valencia and Barcelona. He was named the 2015 Copa América's best defender.
