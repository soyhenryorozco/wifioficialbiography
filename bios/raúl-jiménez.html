Raúl Jiménez (born May 5, 1991), known as Raúl Jiménez, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Toluca, Mexico, Jiménez became a fan favorite at Wolverhampton Wanderers with his goal-scoring exploits. He suffered a serious skull fracture in 2020 but made a remarkable comeback.
