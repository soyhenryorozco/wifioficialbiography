Biography

Han (musician) is a Singer.

Han Ji-sung (Korean: 한지성; born September 14, 2000), known as Han, is a South Korean rapper, singer, songwriter, and producer. He is a member of the South Korean boy band Stray Kids and its production unit 3Racha under JYP Entertainment. Han has been a full member of the Korea Music Copyright Association since February 2023, becoming the youngest K-pop idol to be awarded such membership.
