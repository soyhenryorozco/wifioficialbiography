Roger Martínez (born June 23, 1994), known as Roger Martínez, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Cartagena, Colombia, A versatile forward, Martínez has played for Racing Club, Villarreal, and América in Mexico. He has been a regular for the Colombian national team.
