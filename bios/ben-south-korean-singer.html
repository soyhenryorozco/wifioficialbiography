Biography

Ben (South Korean singer) is a Singer.

Lee Eun-young (Korean: 이은영; born July 30, 1991), better known by her stage name Ben (벤), is a South Korean singer and songwriter. She made her official debut in October 2012, with their first extended play 147.5. As of 2024 she released one studio album, five extended plays and twenty one singles.
