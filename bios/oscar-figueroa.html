Óscar Albeyro Figueroa Mosquera (born April 27, 1983) is a Colombian weightlifter. Born in Zaragoza, Antioquia, he won a silver medal at the 2016 Rio Olympics.

Figueroa is one of Colombia's most decorated athletes, with multiple Pan American Games gold medals and world championship medals. He retired as a national hero after his Olympic performance.
