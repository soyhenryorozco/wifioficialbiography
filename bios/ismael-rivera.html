Ismael Rivera (born October 5, 1931), known as Ismael Rivera, is a puerto rican singer. Born in Santurce, Puerto Rico, Ismael Rivera was a Puerto Rican salsa singer known as El Sonero Mayor. He was one of the most influential figures in salsa music history.
