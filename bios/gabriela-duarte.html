Gabriela Duarte (born April 3, 1974), known as Gabriela Duarte, is a brazilian actress. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Gabriela Duarte is a Brazilian actress known for her roles in telenovelas. She is the daughter of famous actress Regina Duarte.
