Hayley Marie Norman

Hayley Marie Norman

Hayley Marie Norman is an American actress, writer, and creator known for her roles in Kenan, A.P. Bio, Hancock, Chris Rock's Top Five, Adam Ruins Everything, Fired Up!, and Norbit. Norman has written, produced, and starred in a number of online videos and sketches that have been featured by Funny or Die. The digital series she produced and starred in, Hello Cupid, was accepted into Tribeca Film Festival and picked up by BET in 2015.
