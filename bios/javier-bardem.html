Javier Bardem (born March 1, 1969), known as Javier Bardem, is a spanish actor. Born in Las Palmas, Spain, Javier Bardem is a Spanish actor known for No Country for Old Men and Skyfall. He won an Oscar for Best Supporting Actor.
