Richard Carapaz (born May 29, 1993), known as Richard Carapaz, is a ecuadorian professional cyclist. Born in Tulcán, Ecuador, Carapaz won the 2019 Giro d'Italia, becoming the first Ecuadorian to win a Grand Tour. He also won an Olympic gold medal at the 2020 Tokyo Games.
