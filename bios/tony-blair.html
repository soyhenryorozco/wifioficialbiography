Tony Blair (born May 6, 1953), known as Tony Blair, is a british former prime minister of the united kingdom. Born in Edinburgh, Scotland, UK, Tony Blair served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 1997 to 2007, the longest-serving Labour Prime Minister. He led the UK during the Iraq War.
