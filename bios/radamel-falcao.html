Radamel Falcao (born February 10, 1986) is a Colombian professional footballer who plays as a striker. Nicknamed "El Tigre" (The Tiger), he is considered one of the greatest Colombian footballers of all time.

With over 350 goals for club and country, Falcao is the all-time top goalscorer for the Colombia national team. He has played for clubs including River Plate, Porto, Atlético Madrid, Monaco, Manchester United, Chelsea, Galatasaray, and Rayo Vallecano.

Falcao was born in Santa Marta, Magdalena, Colombia. His father, Radamel García, was also a professional footballer. The family moved to Venezuela when Falcao was five, but returned to Colombia in 1995.
