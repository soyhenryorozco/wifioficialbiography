Manuel Turizo Zapata (born April 12, 2000) is a Colombian singer and songwriter. Born in Sincelejo, Sucre, he gained fame with his viral hit 'Una Lady como Tú' which became one of the most-streamed Latin songs.

His music blends reggaeton, pop, and urban genres. He has collaborated with major artists and continues to be a leading figure in Colombian urban music.
