Juan Pablo Montoya (born September 20, 1975), known as Juan Pablo Montoya, is a colombian formula one and racing driver. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Montoya won seven Formula One races and two Monaco Grand Prix. He later competed in NASCAR and IndyCar, becoming one of the most versatile racing drivers in history.
