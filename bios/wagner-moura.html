Wagner Moura (born June 27, 1976), known as Wagner Moura, is a brazilian actor. Born in Salvador, Brazil, Wagner Moura is a Brazilian actor known internationally for portraying Pablo Escobar in Narcos. He has also starred in critically acclaimed Brazilian films.
