Carolina Ramírez

Colombian actress known for La Pola and La Reina del Flow.

ActressColombiaTelenovelaLa Pola

Carolina Ramírez is a Colombian actress best known for her roles in television, particularly her portrayal of revolutionary Policarpa Salavarrieta in La Pola and Yeimi Montoya in La Reina del Flow.

She began her acting career in 2002 and has starred in numerous Colombian television productions including La hija del mariachi, Contra el tiempo, and De brutas, nada.
