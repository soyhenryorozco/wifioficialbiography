Arturo Vidal (born May 22, 1987), known as Arturo Vidal, is a chilean professional footballer. Born in Santiago, Chile, Known as 'El Rey Arturo', Vidal won two Copa América titles with Chile and multiple league titles with Juventus and Bayern Munich. He is known for his aggressive midfield play.
