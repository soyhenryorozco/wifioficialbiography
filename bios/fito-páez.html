Fito Páez (born March 2, 1963), known as Fito Páez, is a argentine singer. Born in Rosario, Argentina, Fito Páez is an Argentine rock musician and pianist known as one of the greatest Latin American rock artists. His emotional lyrics have earned him numerous awards.
