The Weeknd (born February 16, 1990), known as The Weeknd, is a canadian singer and record producer. Born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, The Weeknd is a Canadian singer known for his dark pop and R&B sound, with chart-topping hits like Blinding Lights.
