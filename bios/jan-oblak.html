Jan Oblak (born January 7, 1993), known as Jan Oblak, is a slovenian footballer. Born in Škofja Loka, Slovenia, Jan Oblak has been Atletico Madrid's defensive cornerstone for years, winning multiple Zamora Trophies as La Liga's best goalkeeper.
