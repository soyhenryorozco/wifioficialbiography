Steve Jobs (born February 24, 1955), known as Steve Jobs, is a american co-founder of apple. Born in San Francisco, California, USA, Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Inc. and served as its CEO, leading the development of the Mac, iPhone, and iPad. He transformed multiple industries from personal computing to music.
