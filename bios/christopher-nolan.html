Christopher Nolan (born July 30, 1970), known as Christopher Nolan, is a british-american film director and screenwriter. Born in London, England, UK, Christopher Nolan is an acclaimed film director known for mind-bending films like Inception, The Dark Knight trilogy, and Oppenheimer.
