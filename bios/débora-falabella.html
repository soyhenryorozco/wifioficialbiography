Débora Falabella (born June 22, 1979), known as Débora Falabella, is a brazilian actress. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Débora Falabella is a Brazilian actress known for roles in Central Station and numerous telenovelas. She is one of Brazil's most recognized actresses.
