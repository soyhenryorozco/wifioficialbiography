Scott Morrison (born May 13, 1968), known as Scott Morrison, is a australian former prime minister of australia. Born in Waverley, New South Wales, Australia, Scott Morrison served as Prime Minister of Australia from 2018 to 2022. He led the country through the devastating 2019-2020 bushfire season.
