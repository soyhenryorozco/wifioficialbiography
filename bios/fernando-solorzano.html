Fernando Jose Solorzano Diaz Granados (born June 14, 1972) is a Colombian actor, journalist, and television host. Born in Cartagena de Indias, he studied Social Communication and Journalism at the Universidad Externado de Colombia.

Solorzano has had a diverse career in Colombian media, working as both a journalist and actor. He is recognized for his work on television programs and his contributions to Colombian entertainment.

His career spans over two decades in Colombian television.
