Manuel Neuer (born March 27, 1986), known as Manuel Neuer, is a german footballer. Born in Gelsenkirchen, Germany, Manuel Neuer redefined the goalkeeper position with his sweeper-keeper style, winning the 2014 World Cup and multiple Bundesliga and Champions League titles with Bayern Munich.
