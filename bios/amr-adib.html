Public Figure Latin America

Amr Adib (Arabic: عمرو أديب; born 23 October 1963) is an Egyptian TV presenter and journalist. He is currently working for MBC Masr, where he presents his popular talk show "Al-Hekaya" (الحكاية). In November 2023, Adib announced that he had obtained Saudi citizenship.
