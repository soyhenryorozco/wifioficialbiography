Yuliana Lizarazo

tenista

Carol Yuliana Lizarazo Antolinez (born 23 May 1993) is a Colombian professional tennis player. On 5 January 2015, she reached her highest WTA singles ranking of No. 335 whilst her best doubles ranking was No. 104 on 15 January 2024.
