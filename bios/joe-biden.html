Joe Biden (born November 20, 1942), known as Joe Biden, is a american president of the united states. Born in Scranton, Pennsylvania, USA, Joe Biden is the 46th President of the United States, taking office in 2021. He previously served as Vice President under Barack Obama and as a U.S. Senator from Delaware.
