Nancy Pelosi (born March 26, 1940), known as Nancy Pelosi, is a american former speaker of the u.s. house. Born in Baltimore, Maryland, USA, Nancy Pelosi served as Speaker of the United States House of Representatives from 2007 to 2011 and again from 2019 to 2023. She was the first woman to hold the office.
