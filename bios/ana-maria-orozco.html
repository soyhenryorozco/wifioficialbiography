Ana María Orozco (born July 25, 1973), known as Ana María Orozco, is a colombian actress and tv personality. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Ana María Orozco is a Colombian actress best known for her role as Betty in the internationally acclaimed telenovela 'Yo Soy Betty, La Fea'. She is a beloved figure in Latin American television.
