Paulo Dybala (born November 15, 1993), known as Paulo Dybala, is a argentine footballer. Born in Cordoba, Argentina, Paulo Dybala, known as La Joya, is an Argentine forward celebrated for his left foot, technical ability, and scoring the winning goal in the 2022 World Cup final.
