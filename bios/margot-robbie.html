Margot Robbie (born July 2, 1990), known as Margot Robbie, is a australian actress and producer. Born in Dalby, Queensland, Australia, Margot Robbie is an Australian actress known for her roles in Barbie, The Wolf of Wall Street, and I, Tonya.
