Zharick Andrea Leon Villalba (born November 17, 1976) is a Colombian actress and model. Born in Cartagena de Indias, she is best known for her iconic roles in Colombian telenovelas.

Leon gained fame portraying Rosario Montes in the hit telenovela Pasion de gavilanes and Bella Cepeda in Dona Bella. Her performance in Dona Bella earned her the Latin Pride Award in 2010.

In 2019, she made her film debut in the comedy Los Ajenos Futbol Club.
