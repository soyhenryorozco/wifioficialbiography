Michelle Bachelet (born September 29, 1951), known as Michelle Bachelet, is a chilean politician. Born in Santiago, Chile, Chile's first female president (2006-2010, 2014-2018), Bachelet later served as UN High Commissioner for Human Rights. She was a victim of political persecution under Pinochet's dictatorship.
