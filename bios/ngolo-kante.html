N'Golo Kante (born March 29, 1991), known as N'Golo Kante, is a french footballer. Born in Paris, France, N'Golo Kante is one of the most beloved midfielders in football, winning the Premier League with Leicester City and Chelsea before his star performance in France's 2018 World Cup triumph.
