Didier Drogba (born March 11, 1978), known as Didier Drogba, is a ivorian footballer. Born in Abidjan, Ivory Coast, Didier Drogba scored the decisive penalty in Chelsea's 2012 Champions League final and is celebrated as one of the greatest African footballers in history.
