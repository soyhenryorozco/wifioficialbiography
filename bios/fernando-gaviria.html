Fernando Gaviria Rendón (born August 19, 1994) is a Colombian professional cyclist. Born in La Ceja, Antioquia, he is one of the fastest sprinters in the peloton.

Gaviria has won multiple stages in the Giro d'Italia and Tour de France. He became the first Colombian to wear the pink jersey at the Giro d'Italia.
