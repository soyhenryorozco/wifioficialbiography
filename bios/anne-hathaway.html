Anne Hathaway (born November 12, 1982), known as Anne Hathaway, is a american actress. Born in Brooklyn, New York, USA, Anne Hathaway is an Academy Award-winning actress known for Les Misérables, The Devil Wears Prada, and The Dark Knight Rises.
