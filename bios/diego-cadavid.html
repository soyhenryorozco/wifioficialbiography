Diego Cadavid Sánchez (born December 18, 1978) is a Colombian actor. Born in Santa Marta, Magdalena, he is known for his memorable villain roles in Colombian telenovelas.

Cadavid has become one of the most recognized actors in Colombian television, famous for his intense performances in dramatic roles.
