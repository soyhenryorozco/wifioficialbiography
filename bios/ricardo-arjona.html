Ricardo Arjona (born January 14, 1964), known as Ricardo Arjona, is a guatemalan singer. Born in Guatemala City, Guatemala, Ricardo Arjona is a Guatemalan pop singer known for storytelling lyrics and romantic ballads. He has sold over 20 million records worldwide.
