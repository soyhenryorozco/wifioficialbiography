Jamie Oliver (born May 27, 1975), known as Jamie Oliver, is a british chef and restaurateur. Born in Clavering, Essex, England, UK, Jamie Oliver is a celebrity chef, restaurateur, and cookbook author known for his campaign for healthier school meals.
