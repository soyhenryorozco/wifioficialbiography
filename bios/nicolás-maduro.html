Nicolás Maduro (born November 23, 1962), known as Nicolás Maduro, is a venezuelan politician. Born in Caracas, Venezuela, Maduro succeeded Hugo Chávez as President of Venezuela in 2013. His presidency has been marked by economic crisis, political turmoil, and international sanctions.
