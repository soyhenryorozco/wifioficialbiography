Sebastian Vettel (born July 3, 1987), known as Sebastian Vettel, is a german formula one driver. Born in Heppenheim, Germany, Sebastian Vettel won four consecutive Formula One World Championships from 2010 to 2013 with Red Bull Racing, becoming the youngest champion at age 23.
