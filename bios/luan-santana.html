Luan Santana (born March 13, 1991), known as Luan Santana, is a brazilian singer. Born in Campo Grande, Brazil, Luan Santana is a Brazilian singer known as the King of Sertanejo. He has millions of fans across Brazil and is one of the country's most successful artists.
