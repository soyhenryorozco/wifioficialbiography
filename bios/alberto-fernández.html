Alberto Fernández (born April 2, 1950), known as Alberto Fernández, is a argentine politician. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Fernández served as President of Argentina from 2019 to 2023. He navigated the country through the COVID-19 pandemic and negotiated a major debt restructuring with the IMF.
