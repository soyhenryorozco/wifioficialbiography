Juan Orlando Hernández (born August 28, 1968), known as Juan Orlando Hernández, is a honduran politician. Born in Yoro, Honduras, Hernández served as President of Honduras from 2014 to 2022. He was extradited to the United States on drug trafficking charges in 2022.
