Guillermo Ochoa (born July 13, 1985), known as Guillermo Ochoa, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Guadalajara, Mexico, One of Mexico's most iconic goalkeepers, Ochoa has played in five World Cups and is known for his spectacular saves. He earned the nickname 'Mural de Ochoa'.
