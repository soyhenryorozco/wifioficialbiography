Alejandro Sanz (born December 18, 1968), known as Alejandro Sanz, is a spanish singer. Born in Madrid, Spain, Alejandro Sanz is a Spanish singer-songwriter with 20 Latin Grammy Awards. His flamenco-influenced pop has made him one of Spain's biggest artists.
