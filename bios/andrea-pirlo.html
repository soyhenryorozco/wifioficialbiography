Andrea Pirlo (born May 19, 1979), known as Andrea Pirlo, is a italian footballer. Born in Flero, Italy, Andrea Pirlo reinvented the deep-lying playmaker role, winning two Champions League titles and the 2006 World Cup with his signature no-look passes and vision.
