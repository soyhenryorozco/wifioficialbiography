Ana Paula Arósio (born June 16, 1975), known as Ana Paula Arósio, is a brazilian actress. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Ana Paula Arósio is a Brazilian actress and model known for roles in Brazilian telenovelas. She has been one of Brazil's most popular actresses.
