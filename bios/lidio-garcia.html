Public Figure Latin America

Lidio Arturo García Turbay (born 10 February 1971) is a Colombian politician serving as a member of the Senate since 2010. He has served as president of the Senate since 2025, having previously served from 2019 to 2020. From 2006 to 2010, he was a member of the Chamber of Representatives.
