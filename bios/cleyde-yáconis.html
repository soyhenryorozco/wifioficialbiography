Cleyde Yáconis (born October 19, 1928), known as Cleyde Yáconis, is a brazilian actress. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Cleyde Yáconis was a Brazilian actress considered one of the greatest in Brazilian cinema history. She passed away in 2013 at age 84.
