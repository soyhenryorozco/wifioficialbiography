Irandhir Santos (born August 22, 1975), known as Irandhir Santos, is a brazilian actor. Born in Salvador, Brazil, Irandhir Santos is a Brazilian actor known for powerful performances in City of God and Elite Squad. He is one of Brazil's most intense actors.
