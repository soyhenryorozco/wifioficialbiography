Marc Anthony (born September 16, 1968), known as Marc Anthony, is a american-puerto rican singer. Born in New York City, United States, Marc Anthony is an American-born Puerto Rican salsa and pop singer. He is one of the best-selling salsa artists of all time with multiple Grammy Awards.
