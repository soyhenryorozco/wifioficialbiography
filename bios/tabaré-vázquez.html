Tabaré Vázquez (born January 17, 1949), known as Tabaré Vázquez, is a uruguayan politician and physician. Born in Montevideo, Uruguay, Vázquez served two terms as President of Uruguay (2005-2010, 2015-2020). He was the first left-wing president in Uruguayan history and implemented significant social reforms.
