Jair Bolsonaro (born March 21, 1955), known as Jair Bolsonaro, is a brazilian politician. Born in Glicério, Brazil, A far-right former military officer, Bolsonaro served as President of Brazil from 2019 to 2022. His presidency was controversial for its stance on the Amazon rainforest and COVID-19.
