Rigoberto Urán Urán (born January 26, 1986) is a Colombian professional cyclist. Born in Urrao, Antioquia, he won a silver medal at the 2012 London Olympics in the road race.

Urán has been a prominent figure in the Tour de France, finishing second overall in 2013 and 2014. He is known for his climbing ability and charismatic personality.
