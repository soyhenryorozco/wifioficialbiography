Jozias van Aartsen (born December 25, 1947) is a politician, diplomat. Jozias Johannes van Aartsen (Dutch: [ˈjoːzijɑ ɕoːˈɦɑnəs fɑn ˈaːrtsə(n)]; born 25 December 1947) is a retired Dutch politician who served as Leader of the People's Party for Freedom and Democracy

A native of The Hague, he attended the Christelijk Gymnasium Sorghvliet from April 1960 until May 1968 and applied at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam in June 1968 majoring in Law and obtaining a Bachelor of Laws degree in July 1970. Van Aartsen served as Minister of Agriculture, Nature and Fisherie
