Theresa May (born October 1, 1956), known as Theresa May, is a british former prime minister of the united kingdom. Born in Eastbourne, England, UK, Theresa May served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 2016 to 2019. She was the second female Prime Minister of the UK after Margaret Thatcher.
