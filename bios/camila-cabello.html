Camila Cabello (born March 3, 1997), known as Camila Cabello, is a american-cuban singer. Born in Havana, Cuba, Camila Cabello is an American-Cuban singer who rose to fame as a member of Fifth Harmony. Her solo hit Havana topped charts worldwide.
