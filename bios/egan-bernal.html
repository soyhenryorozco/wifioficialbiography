Egan Arley Bernal Martínez (born January 26, 1997) is a Colombian professional cyclist. Born in Bogotá, he became the first Colombian to win the Tour de France in 2019.

Bernal's victory was a historic moment for Colombian sports. He also won the Giro d'Italia in 2021 and has been one of the most talented young riders in professional cycling.
