Lilly Singh (born September 26, 1988), known as Lilly Singh, is a canadian youtuber and comedian. Born in Scarborough, Ontario, Canada, Lilly Singh is a Canadian YouTuber, comedian, and actress known for her channel IISuperwomanII. She hosted her own late-night talk show A Little Late with Lilly Singh.
