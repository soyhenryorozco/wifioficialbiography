Ja Morant (born August 10, 1999), known as Ja Morant, is a american basketball player. Born in Dalzell, South Carolina, USA, Ja Morant won the 2020 Rookie of the Year award and became one of the most electrifying players in the NBA with his high-flying dunks with the Memphis Grizzlies.
