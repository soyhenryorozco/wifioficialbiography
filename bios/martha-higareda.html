Martha Elba Higareda Cervantes (born August 24, 1983) is a Mexican actress, producer, and screenwriter. Born in Villahermosa, Tabasco, she is one of Mexico most internationally recognized actresses.

Higareda gained international fame for her role as Kristin Ortega in the Netflix science fiction series Altered Carbon (2018-2020). She also appeared in Queen of the South.

She has starred in numerous Mexican films and telenovelas, and is also a successful producer and screenwriter.
