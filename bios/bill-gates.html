Bill Gates (born October 28, 1955), known as Bill Gates, is a american co-founder of microsoft. Born in Seattle, Washington, USA, Bill Gates co-founded Microsoft and led it to become the world's largest personal computer software company. He now focuses on philanthropy through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
