Nairo Alexander Quintana Rojas (born February 7, 1990) is a Colombian professional cyclist. Born in Tunja, Boyacá, he is one of the most successful Colombian cyclists in history.

Quintana won the Giro d'Italia in 2014 and the Tour de l'Avenir. He has also finished second in the Tour de France and the Vuelta a España, establishing himself as one of the great climbers in professional cycling.
