Brandon Flowers (born June 21, 1981), known as Brandon Flowers, is a american singer and songwriter. Born in Henderson, United States, Brandon Flowers is the lead vocalist of The Killers, known for 'Mr. Brightside' and 'Somebody Told Me'.
