Whinderssonnunes (born January 5, 1995), known as Whinderssonnunes, is a brazilian youtuber and comedian. Born in Marabá, Brazil, Whindersson is one of Brazil's biggest YouTubers with over 40 million subscribers. He creates comedy sketches, vlogs, and has expanded into music and film.
