Bob Clearmountain (born January 16, 1953) is an mixing engineer and record producer. Bob Clearmountain (né Chiaramonte; January 15, 1953) is an American mixing engineer and record producer, best known for his work with major acts, including Bryan Adams, David Bowie, Bon Jovi, Huey Le

Clearmountain has mixed some of the most famous live shows in music history, including Live Aid and The Concert for New York City. Records mixed by Clearmountain have won eight Grammy Awards. He has also won two Emmy Awards from five nominations.
