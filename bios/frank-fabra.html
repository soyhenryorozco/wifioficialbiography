Frank Fabra (born February 7, 1991), known as Frank Fabra, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Turbo, Colombia, An attacking left-back, Fabra won the Copa Libertadores with Boca Juniors and has been a regular for Colombia's national team with his overlapping runs.
