Biography

Alon De Loco is a Singer.

Alon Cohen (Hebrew: אלון כהן, born November 25, 1974), known professionally by his stage name Alon De Loco, is an Israeli singer, rapper and record producer. De Loco is one of the leading artists of the Israeli reggaeton.
