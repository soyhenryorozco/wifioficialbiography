Héctor Lavoe (born September 30, 1946), known as Héctor Lavoe, is a puerto rican singer. Born in Ponce, Puerto Rico, Héctor Lavoe was a Puerto Rican salsa singer known as El Cantante de los Cantantes. He is considered one of the most iconic figures in salsa music.
