Daddy Yankee (born February 3, 1977), known as Daddy Yankee, is a puerto rican singer. Born in Río Piedras, Puerto Rico, Daddy Yankee is a Puerto Rican reggaeton pioneer often called the King of Reggaeton. His hit Gasolina helped popularize the genre worldwide.
