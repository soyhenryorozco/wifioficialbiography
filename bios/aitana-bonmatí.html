Aitana Bonmatí (born June 18, 1998), known as Aitana Bonmatí, is a spanish professional footballer. Born in Sant Celoni, Spain, Aitana Bonmatí won the 2023 Ballon d'Or Féminin and was key to Spain's World Cup win.
