Jorge Celedón (born August 23, 1968), known as Jorge Celedón, is a colombian singer. Born in Valledupar, Colombia, Jorge Celedón is a Colombian vallenato singer known for his traditional style. He has won Latin Grammy Awards and is a leading figure in vallenato music.
