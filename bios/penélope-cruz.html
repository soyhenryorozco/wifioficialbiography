Penélope Cruz (born April 28, 1974), known as Penélope Cruz, is a spanish actress. Born in Madrid, Spain, Penélope Cruz is a Spanish actress known for her collaborations with Pedro Almodóvar. She won an Oscar for Vicky Cristina Barcelona.
