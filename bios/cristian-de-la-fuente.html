Cristian de la Fuente (born October 10, 1974), known as Cristian de la Fuente, is a chilean actor. Born in Santiago, Chile, Cristian de la Fuente is a Chilean actor and producer known for roles in In the Heights, Drive Angry, and numerous telenovelas.
