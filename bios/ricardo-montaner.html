Ricardo Montaner (born November 8, 1956), known as Ricardo Montaner, is a venezuelan-argentine singer. Born in Valencia, Venezuela, Ricardo Montaner is a Venezuelan-Argentine pop singer known for romantic ballads. He has sold over 20 million records over a decades-long career.
