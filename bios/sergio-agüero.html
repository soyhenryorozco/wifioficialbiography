Sergio Agüero (born June 2, 1988), known as Sergio Agüero, is a argentine professional footballer. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Agüero scored the most iconic goal in Premier League history to win the 2012 title for Manchester City. He is City's all-time leading scorer and Argentina's fourth-highest scorer.
