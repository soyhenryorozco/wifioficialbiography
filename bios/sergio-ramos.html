Sergio Ramos (born March 30, 1986), known as Sergio Ramos, is a spanish footballer. Born in Camas, Spain, Sergio Ramos has won four Champions League titles with Real Madrid and captained Spain to a World Cup victory in 2010, known for his leadership and crucial goals from defense.
