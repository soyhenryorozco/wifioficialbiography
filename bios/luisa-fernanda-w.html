Luisa Fernanda W (born June 4, 1999) is a Colombian YouTube content creator known for her vlogs, storytimes, beauty content, and lifestyle videos.

Luisa began her YouTube career in 2015, initially creating videos about her daily life, beauty routines, and personal experiences. Her authentic personality and relatable content quickly resonated with Colombian and Latin American audiences.

Her channel grew significantly through her engaging storytelling style, covering topics from relationships and family life to travel and beauty tutorials. She became one of Colombia's most popular female YouTubers, amassing millions of subscribers and views.

Luisa's content is characterized by its casual, conversational tone, making viewers feel like they're chatting with a friend. She frequently collaborates with other Colombian influencers and has worked with major brands in fashion and beauty.

Early Life

Luisa Fernanda Woolfolk was born on June 4, 1999 in Colombia. She grew up in a close-knit family and began sharing her life on social media during her teenage years. Her natural charisma and openness about her experiences made her an instant hit with viewers.

Career

Luisa's YouTube channel features a mix of vlogs, beauty content, storytimes, and lifestyle videos. Her most popular series include "Storytime" videos where she shares personal anecdotes, and "Get Ready With Me" videos featuring beauty and fashion content.

She has collaborated with numerous brands including fashion, beauty, and lifestyle companies. Luisa has also participated in various events and campaigns across Colombia and Latin America.
