Yerry Mina (born September 23, 1994) is a Colombian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Cagliari and the Colombia national team.

Mina became the first Colombian to play for FC Barcelona in 2018. At the 2018 FIFA World Cup, he scored three goals, tying the record for most goals scored in a single World Cup by a defender.
