Natanael Cano (born May 1, 2001), known as Natanael Cano, is a mexican singer. Born in Sonora, Mexico, Natanael Cano is a Mexican singer known for popularizing corridos tumbados, a fusion of traditional Mexican corridos with trap and hip-hop.
