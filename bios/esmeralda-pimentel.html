Esmeralda Pimentel (born September 8, 1989), known as Esmeralda Pimentel, is a mexican actress. Born in Jalisco, Mexico, Esmeralda Pimentel is a Mexican actress and model known for telenovela and film roles. She has won the TVyNovelas Award for Best Actress.
