Denise Rosenthal (born November 8, 1990), known as Denise Rosenthal, is a chilean singer. Born in Santiago, Chile, Denise Rosenthal is a Chilean singer known for empowering pop anthems. She has been a vocal advocate for women's rights and LGBTQ+ issues.
