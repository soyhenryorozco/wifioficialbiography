Patrick Vieira (born June 23, 1976), known as Patrick Vieira, is a french footballer and manager. Born in Dakar, Senegal, Patrick Vieira was the midfield engine of Arsenal's Invincibles season and captained France to the 2006 World Cup final before transitioning into management.
