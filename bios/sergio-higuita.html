Sergio Higuita (born August 1, 1997), known as Sergio Higuita, is a colombian professional cyclist. Born in Medellín, Colombia, A talented climbing specialist, Higuita won the Tour of Flanders and has been a strong performer in WorldTour races. He joined BORA-Hansgrohe from EF Education.
