Diana Turbay Quintero (born March 8, 1950) was a Colombian journalist. Born in Bogotá, she was kidnapped by the Medellín Cartel in 1990 and killed during a rescue attempt in 1991.

Her death shocked Colombia and brought international attention to the country's drug-related violence. She remains a symbol of press freedom.
