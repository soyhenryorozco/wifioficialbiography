Robert Lewandowski (born August 21, 1988), known as Robert Lewandowski, is a polish professional footballer. Born in Warsaw, Poland, Lewandowski broke Gerd Müller's long-standing Bundesliga scoring record with 41 goals in a season. He joined Barcelona in 2022 after a legendary spell at Bayern Munich.
