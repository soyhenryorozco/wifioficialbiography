Chiara Ferragni (born 1987-05-07), known as Chiara Ferragni, is a italian fashion blogger and influencer. Born in Cremona, Italy, Chiara Ferragni is an Italian fashion influencer and entrepreneur behind The Blonde Salad, one of the first fashion blogs to go global.
