Lina Marulanda Arbeláez (born April 25, 1980) was a Colombian model and television presenter. Born in Medellín, Antioquia, she was one of Colombia's most beloved media figures.

Marulanda tragically passed away in 2010 but remains a beloved figure in Colombian media and pop culture.
