Salma Hayek (born September 2, 1966), known as Salma Hayek, is a mexican-american actress. Born in Coatzacoalcos, Mexico, Salma Hayek is a Mexican-American actress and producer who broke barriers for Latinas in Hollywood. She received an Oscar nomination for Frida.
