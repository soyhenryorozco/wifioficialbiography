Anthony Hopkins (born December 31, 1937), known as Anthony Hopkins, is a welsh-british actor. Born in Port Talbot, Wales, Sir Anthony Hopkins is a two-time Academy Award-winning Welsh actor known for The Silence of the Lambs.
