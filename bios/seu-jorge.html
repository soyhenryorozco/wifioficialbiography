Seu Jorge (born June 8, 1970), known as Seu Jorge, is a brazilian actor. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Seu Jorge is a Brazilian actor and musician known for his role in City of God and his David Bowie tribute album in The Life Aquatic.
