Juliana Galvis

Colombian actress and model known for her roles in telenovelas.

ActressColombiaModelTelenovela

Juliana Galvis is a Colombian actress and model known for her roles in various Colombian telenovelas and television series. She has appeared in productions such as Pura Sangre and El Secretario.

Galvis began her career in modeling before transitioning to acting, where she quickly gained recognition for her talent and versatility.
