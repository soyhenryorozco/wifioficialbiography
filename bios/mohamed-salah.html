Mohamed Salah (born June 15, 1992), known as Mohamed Salah, is a egyptian professional footballer. Born in Nagrig, Egypt, Salah has become Liverpool's all-time Premier League scorer and won the Champions League and Premier League. He is the first Egyptian to win the PFA Players' Player of the Year.
