Carmelo Anthony (born May 29, 1984), known as Carmelo Anthony, is a american basketball player. Born in Brooklyn, New York, USA, Carmelo Anthony is a 10-time NBA All-Star and three-time Olympic gold medalist, ranking among the top 10 scorers in NBA history with his elite shot-making ability.
