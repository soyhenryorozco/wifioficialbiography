Andrés Felipe Gómez (born April 18, 1992), known as Andrés Felipe Gómez, is a colombian youtuber and content creator. Born in Bogotá, Colombia, Andrés Felipe Gómez is a Colombian YouTuber known for his travel and lifestyle content. He shares adventures from across Colombia and Latin America.
