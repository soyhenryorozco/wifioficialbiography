Shinzo Abe (born September 21, 1954), known as Shinzo Abe, is a japanese former prime minister of japan. Born in Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan, Shinzo Abe served as Prime Minister of Japan from 2006 to 2007 and again from 2012 to 2020. He was the longest-serving Prime Minister in Japanese history.
