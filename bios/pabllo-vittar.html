Pabllo Vittar (born November 1, 1994), known as Pabllo Vittar, is a brazilian singer. Born in São Luís, Brazil, Pabllo Vittar is a Brazilian singer and drag queen who has broken barriers as one of the most successful LGBTQ+ artists in Latin America.
