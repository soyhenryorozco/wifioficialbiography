Ederson Moraes (born August 17, 1993), known as Ederson Moraes, is a brazilian footballer. Born in Osasco, Brazil, Ederson has redefined goalkeeping at Manchester City with his extraordinary distribution and shot-stopping, winning multiple Premier League titles under Pep Guardiola.
