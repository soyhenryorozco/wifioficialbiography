Luis Manuel Cardona Gardín (born April 25, 1977), known as Manolo Cardona, is a Colombian actor and producer. Born in Popayán, Cauca, he is known for international Netflix productions.

Cardona starred in Who Killed Sara? (¿Quién mató a Sara?), which became one of Netflix's most-watched Spanish-language series globally.
