Jhon Córdoba (born May 11, 1993), known as Jhon Córdoba, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Istmina, Colombia, A powerful striker, Córdoba moved from Eintracht Frankfurt to Krasnodar and then to Hertha Berlin. He has been a regular goal scorer in European football.
