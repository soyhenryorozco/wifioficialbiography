Andre Agassi (born April 29, 1970), known as Andre Agassi, is a american tennis player. Born in Las Vegas, Nevada, USA, Andre Agassi won eight Grand Slam titles and completed a Career Golden Slam, known for his powerful return game and iconic persona both on and off the court.
