Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva (born October 27, 1945), known as Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva, is a brazilian president of brazil. Born in Caetes, Pernambuco, Brazil, Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva is the President of Brazil, serving since 2023 after previously holding office from 2003 to 2010. He is a founding member of the Workers Party.
