Catalina Sandino Moreno (born April 19, 1981) is a Colombian actress. Born in Bogotá, she received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress for her debut role in Maria Full of Grace (2004).

She is one of the few Colombian actors to receive an Oscar nomination and has continued working in international cinema.
