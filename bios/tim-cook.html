Tim Cook (born November 1, 1960), known as Tim Cook, is a american ceo of apple. Born in Robertsdale, Alabama, USA, Tim Cook has served as CEO of Apple since 2011, succeeding Steve Jobs. He previously served as Apple's Chief Operating Officer and is known for his supply chain expertise.
