César Augusto Gaviria Trujillo (born December 31, 1947) is a Colombian politician who served as President of Colombia from 1990 to 1994. Born in Pereira, Risaralda.

Gaviria implemented major economic and political reforms during his presidency and has remained an influential figure in Colombian and Latin American politics.
