Gustavo Kuerten (born September 10, 1976), known as Gustavo Kuerten, is a brazilian professional tennis player. Born in Florianópolis, Brazil, Known as 'Guga', Kuerten won three French Open titles and reached world number one. He became a national hero in Brazil and was beloved for his charismatic personality.
