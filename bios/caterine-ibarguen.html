Caterine Ibargüen Mena (born February 12, 1984) is a Colombian triple jumper. Born in Apartadó, Antioquia, she won the gold medal at the 2016 Rio Olympics.

Ibargüen is one of Colombia's most celebrated athletes, with Olympic gold, World Championship titles, and Pan American Games records. She is an inspiration for Colombian women in sports.
