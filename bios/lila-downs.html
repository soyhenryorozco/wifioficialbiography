Lila Downs (born September 9, 1968), known as Lila Downs, is a mexican-american singer. Born in Tlaxiaco, Mexico, Lila Downs is a Mexican-American singer known for blending traditional Mexican and indigenous music with modern styles. She has won multiple Grammy Awards.
