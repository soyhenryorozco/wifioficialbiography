Florian Wirtz (born May 3, 2003), known as Florian Wirtz, is a german footballer. Born in Pulheim, Germany, Florian Wirtz has become the centerpiece of Bayer Leverkusen's historic unbeaten Bundesliga season, regarded as one of the most talented young players in the world.
