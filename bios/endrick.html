Endrick is a brazilian footballer.

Endrick Felipe Moreira de Sousa Pessoa (born 21 July 2006), known mononymously as Endrick (Brazilian Portuguese: [ˈẽ(j̃)dɾik(i)]), is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a striker for La Liga club Real Madrid and the Brazil national team.
