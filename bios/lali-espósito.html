Lali Espósito (born October 10, 1991), known as Lali Espósito, is a argentine singer. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Lali Espósito is an Argentine singer and actress who rose to fame with Teen Angels. She is now one of Argentina's biggest pop stars.
