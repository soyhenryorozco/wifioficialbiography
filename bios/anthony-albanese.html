Anthony Albanese (born March 2, 1963), known as Anthony Albanese, is a australian prime minister of australia. Born in Darlinghurst, New South Wales, Australia, Anthony Albanese has served as Prime Minister of Australia since 2022. He is the leader of the Australian Labor Party.
