Tiësto (born October 29, 1979), known as Tiësto, is a dutch dj. Born in Breda, Netherlands, Tiësto is a Dutch DJ and record producer who has collaborated with major Latin artists. He has been named the Greatest DJ of All Time by Mix magazine.
