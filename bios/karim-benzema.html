Karim Benzema (born December 19, 1987), known as Karim Benzema, is a french footballer. Born in Lyon, France, Karim Benzema won the 2022 Ballon d'Or after a phenomenal season with Real Madrid, finishing as the Champions League's top scorer and leading La Liga in goals.
