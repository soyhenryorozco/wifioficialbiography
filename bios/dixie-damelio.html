Dixie D'Amelio (born August 12, 2001), known as Dixie D'Amelio, is a american singer and social media personality. Born in Norwalk, Connecticut, USA, Dixie D'Amelio is a singer and social media personality who rose to fame alongside her sister Charli on TikTok. She has released multiple singles and an EP.
