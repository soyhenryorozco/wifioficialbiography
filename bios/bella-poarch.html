Bella Poarch (born February 8, 1997), known as Bella Poarch, is a filipino-american singer and tiktok personality. Born in San Manuel, Pangasinan, Philippines, Bella Poarch is a Filipino-American singer and TikTok personality who became famous for her lip-sync videos. Her single Build a Bitch went viral globally.
