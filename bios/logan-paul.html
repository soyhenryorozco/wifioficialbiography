Logan Paul (born April 1, 1995), known as Logan Paul, is a american youtuber and boxer. Born in Westlake, Ohio, USA, Logan Paul is a YouTuber, actor, and professional boxer. He rose to fame on Vine before transitioning to YouTube and later co-founding the sports drink Prime with KSI.
