Virgil van Dijk (born July 8, 1991), known as Virgil van Dijk, is a dutch footballer. Born in Breda, Netherlands, Virgil van Dijk transformed Liverpool's defense upon his arrival and has been named UEFA Men's Player of the Year, establishing himself as the world's best center-back.
