MrBeast (born May 7, 1998), known as MrBeast, is a american youtuber and philanthropist. Born in Wichita, Kansas, USA, MrBeast, whose real name is Jimmy Donaldson, is the most-subscribed individual YouTuber known for his expensive stunts and philanthropic giveaways. He founded Feastables and TeamTrees.
