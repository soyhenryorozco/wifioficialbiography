No Te Va Gustar (born 1994), known as No Te Va Gustar, is a uruguayan rock band. Born in Montevideo, Uruguay, No Te Va Gustar is a Uruguayan rock band known for blending rock, reggae, and Latin rhythms. They have become one of the most successful bands in the region.
