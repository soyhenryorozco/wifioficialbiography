Paulina Rubio (born June 17, 1971), known as Paulina Rubio, is a mexican singer. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Paulina Rubio is a Mexican pop singer known as La Chica Dorada. She was a member of Timbiriche before launching a successful solo career.
