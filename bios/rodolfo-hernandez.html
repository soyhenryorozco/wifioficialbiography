Rodolfo Hernández Suárez (born October 1, 1961) is a Colombian politician and engineer. Born in Sucre, Santander, he became a viral TikTok sensation during the 2022 presidential campaign.

Hernández was the runner-up in the 2022 Colombian presidential election, gaining massive social media support.
