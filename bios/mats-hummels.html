Mats Hummels (born December 16, 1988), known as Mats Hummels, is a german footballer. Born in Bergisch Gladbach, Germany, Mats Hummels has been one of the best ball-playing center-backs of his era, winning the World Cup with Germany in 2014 and multiple Bundesliga titles with Borussia Dortmund.
