Carla Morrison (born July 19, 1986), known as Carla Morrison, is a mexican singer. Born in Tecate, Mexico, Carla Morrison is a Mexican indie pop singer known for her ethereal voice and emotional songwriting. She has been nominated for multiple Latin Grammy Awards.
