Gustavo Cerati (born May 2, 1959), known as Gustavo Cerati, is a argentine singer. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Gustavo Cerati was an Argentine rock musician, the frontman of Soda Stereo and a solo artist. He is considered one of the most influential Latin American rock musicians.
