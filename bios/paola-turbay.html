Paola Turbay Gómez (born November 2, 1970) is a Colombian actress, model, and television presenter. Born in Bogotá, she was crowned Miss Colombia 1991 and was first runner-up at Miss Universe 1992.

Turbay has become one of Colombia's most recognizable television presenters and actresses.
