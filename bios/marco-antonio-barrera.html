Marco Antonio Barrera (born January 17, 1974), known as Marco Antonio Barrera, is a mexican professional boxer. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Barrera won world titles in five weight divisions and is known for his fierce rivalry with Érik Morales. He was inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame.
