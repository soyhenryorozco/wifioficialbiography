Lewis Hamilton (born January 7, 1985), known as Lewis Hamilton, is a british formula one driver. Born in Stevenage, England, Lewis Hamilton is a seven-time Formula One World Champion, tied with Michael Schumacher for the most titles, and holds the records for most wins and pole positions.
