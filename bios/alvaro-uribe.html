Álvaro Uribe Vélez (born July 4, 1952) is a Colombian politician who served as President of Colombia from 2002 to 2010. Born in Medellín, Antioquia, he implemented the Democratic Security policy.

Uribe remains one of the most influential and controversial figures in modern Colombian politics.
