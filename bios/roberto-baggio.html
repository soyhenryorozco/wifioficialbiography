Roberto Baggio (born February 18, 1967), known as Roberto Baggio, is a italian footballer. Born in Caldogno, Italy, Known as the Divine Ponytail, Roberto Baggio was an Italian attacking midfielder celebrated for his vision, creativity, and iconic penalty miss in the 1994 World Cup final.
