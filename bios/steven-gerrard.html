Steven Gerrard (born May 30, 1980), known as Steven Gerrard, is a english footballer and manager. Born in Merseyside, England, Steven Gerrard is Liverpool's greatest captain, famous for his inspiring 2005 Champions League final performance and his powerful long-range goals.
