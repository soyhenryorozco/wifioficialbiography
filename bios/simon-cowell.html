Simon Cowell (born October 7, 1959), known as Simon Cowell, is a british television producer and talent judge. Born in London, England, UK, Simon Cowell is a television producer and talent show judge known for his blunt critiques on American Idol, The X Factor, and Britain's Got Talent.
