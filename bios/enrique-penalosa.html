Enrique Peñalosa Londoño (born September 7, 1957) is a Colombian politician and urbanist. Born in Washington D.C. to Colombian parents, he served as mayor of Bogotá.

Peñalosa is known for transforming Bogotá's infrastructure, implementing the TransMilenio bus rapid transit system and expanding the city's cycling network.
