Leopoldo Pacheco (born November 2, 1963), known as Leopoldo Pacheco, is a brazilian actor. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Leopoldo Pacheco is a Brazilian actor known for his villainous roles in telenovelas and films. He is one of Brazil's most recognizable character actors.
