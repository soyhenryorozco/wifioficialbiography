Rolf Aagaard-Svendsen

Rolf Aagaard-Svendsen (born 28 March 1948 in Hellerup) is a Danish politician, who served as mayor of Lyngby-Taarbæk Municipality from 2002 to 2010, elected for Conservative People's Party.
