La Lerma (La Lerma) is a Colombian content creator, tiktoker, dancer, musician. Colombian content creator based in Cartagena known for viral dance content, comedy sketches, and trending challenges with high engagement rates.

Early life and background

La Lerma is a Colombian content creator from the Cartagena/Barranquilla area who rose to prominence through viral dance and comedy content.

Career

La Lerma is a Colombian content creator based in Cartagena known for viral dance content, comedy sketches, and trending challenges. She frequently collaborates with partner Freyder Cantillo and other top Colombian TikTokers.

Ranked #27 in the B-Fun 2025 Digital Influence Index, she has built a significant following through her high engagement rates and energetic, personality-driven content.

Her Instagram account @lermamusic_ has over 1.5M followers with an engagement rate of approximately 2.7%, higher than most creators. She continues to be a leading force in the Colombian TikTok dance and comedy scene.
