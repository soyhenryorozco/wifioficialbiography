Lucho Bermúdez (born January 23, 1912), known as Lucho Bermúdez, is a colombian composer. Born in Toribío, Colombia, Lucho Bermúdez was a Colombian composer and musician known as the king of porro and gaita. He modernized Colombian folk music and influenced generations.
