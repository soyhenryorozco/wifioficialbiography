Luis Gerardo Méndez (born March 12, 1982), known as Luis Gerardo Méndez, is a mexican actor. Born in Jalisco, Mexico, Luis Gerardo Méndez is a Mexican actor and producer known for Nosotros los Nobles and Club de Cuervos. He is one of Mexico's most popular actors.
