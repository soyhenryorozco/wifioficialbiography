Manny Pacquiao (born December 17, 1978), known as Manny Pacquiao, is a philippine boxer and politician. Born in Bukidnon, Philippines, Manny Pacquiao is the only boxer to win world titles in eight different weight divisions and later became a senator and presidential candidate in the Philippines.
