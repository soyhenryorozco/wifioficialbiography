Denis Villeneuve (born October 3, 1967), known as Denis Villeneuve, is a canadian film director and producer. Born in Trois-Rivieres, Quebec, Canada, Denis Villeneuve is a Canadian film director known for epic sci-fi films including Arrival, Blade Runner 2049, and Dune.
