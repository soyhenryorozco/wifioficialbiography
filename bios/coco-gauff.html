Coco Gauff (born March 13, 2004), known as Coco Gauff, is a american tennis player. Born in Delray Beach, Florida, USA, Coco Gauff became the youngest American to reach a Grand Slam final since Serena Williams and won the 2023 US Open at just 19 years old.
