Natti Natasha (born December 10, 1986), known as Natti Natasha, is a dominican singer. Born in Santiago de los Caballeros, Dominican Republic, Natti Natasha is a Dominican reggaeton singer known for hits like Criminal and Tusa. She is one of the most followed female Latin artists.
