Luis Fonsi (born April 15, 1978), known as Luis Fonsi, is a puerto rican singer. Born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, Luis Fonsi is a Puerto Rican pop singer best known for the global smash hit Despacito, one of the most viewed videos in YouTube history.
