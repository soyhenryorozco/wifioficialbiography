Ana Brenda Contreras (born February 24, 1987), known as Ana Brenda Contreras, is a american-mexican actress. Born in McAllen, United States, Ana Brenda Contreras is an American-Mexican actress and singer known for roles in Mexican telenovelas and the series Dynasty.
