Biography

Diego Torres (singer) is a Singer.

Diego Antonio Caccia, known as Diego Torres, (born 9 March 1971) is an Argentine pop singer and actor. His music is generally categorized as Latin pop, tropical, and rock and roll. He is a member of the Latin Songwriters Hall of Fame, and the son of the Argentine icon Lolita Torres. His work has been well received throughout Latin America and he is a recipient of numerous awards, including the Latin Grammy Award, which he has won three times.
