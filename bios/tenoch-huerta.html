Tenoch Huerta (born January 29, 1981), known as Tenoch Huerta, is a mexican actor. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Tenoch Huerta is a Mexican actor known for his role as Namor in Black Panther: Wakanda Forever. He has been acclaimed for powerful performances.
