Khaled Hosseini (born March 4, 1965), known as Khaled Hosseini, is a afghan-american author. Born in Kabul, Afghanistan, Khaled Hosseini's The Kite Runner became an international bestseller, bringing Afghan stories to a global audience and raising awareness through his foundation.
