Biography

Bora (singer) is a Singer.

Kim Bo-ra (Korean: 김보라; born March 3, 1999), known mononymously as Bora, is a South Korean singer and actress. She is a former member of the South Korean girl group Cherry Bullet. She is best known for her participation in Mnet's reality survival show Girls Planet 999.
