Rafael Nadal (born June 3, 1986), known as Rafael Nadal, is a spanish professional tennis player. Born in Manacor, Mallorca, Spain, Rafael Nadal is known as the King of Clay with a record 14 French Open titles and 22 Grand Slam singles titles.
