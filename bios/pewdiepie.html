PewDiePie (born October 24, 1989), known as PewDiePie, is a swedish youtuber. Born in Gothenburg, Sweden, PewDiePie, born Felix Kjellberg, is one of the most subscribed individual YouTubers, known for his video game commentaries and meme reviews. He has been a dominant force on the platform since 2010.
