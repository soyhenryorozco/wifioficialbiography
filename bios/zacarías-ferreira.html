Zacarías Ferreira (born July 30, 1979), known as Zacarías Ferreira, is a dominican singer. Born in Moca, Dominican Republic, Zacarías Ferreira is a Dominican bachata singer known for romantic ballads. He has become one of the most popular bachata artists of his generation.
