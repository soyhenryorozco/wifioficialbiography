Andrés Parra

Colombian actor best known for portraying Pablo Escobar in TV series.

ActorColombiaTelevisionPablo Escobar

Andrés Parra is a Colombian actor best known for his portrayal of Pablo Escobar in the TV series Escobar, el patrón del mal (2012). His performance earned him widespread recognition across Latin America.

Parra has appeared in numerous Colombian and international television productions and films. He is considered one of Colombia's most talented actors.
