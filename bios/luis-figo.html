Luis Figo (born November 4, 1972), known as Luis Figo, is a portuguese footballer. Born in Almada, Portugal, Luis Figo's controversial transfer from Barcelona to Real Madrid remains one of football's most infamous moves, and he won the 2000 Ballon d'Or.
