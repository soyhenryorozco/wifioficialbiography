Fernanda Tapia (born November 22, 1994), known as Fernanda Tapia, is a mexican youtuber and content creator. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Tapia is a Mexican YouTuber who creates lifestyle and entertainment content. She has built a following through vlogs, challenges, and collaborations with other creators.
