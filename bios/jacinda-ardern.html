Jacinda Ardern (born July 26, 1980), known as Jacinda Ardern, is a new zealander former prime minister of new zealand. Born in Hamilton, New Zealand, Jacinda Ardern served as Prime Minister of New Zealand from 2017 to 2023. She gained international acclaim for her handling of the Christchurch mosque shootings and the COVID-19 pandemic.
