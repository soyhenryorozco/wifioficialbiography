Selton Mello (born December 30, 1972), known as Selton Mello, is a brazilian actor. Born in Passo Fundo, Brazil, Selton Mello is a Brazilian actor and director known for versatile performances in film and television. He has won the Gramado Film Festival Award multiple times.
