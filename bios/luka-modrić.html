Luka Modrić (born September 9, 1985), known as Luka Modrić, is a croatian professional footballer. Born in Zadar, Croatia, The 2018 Ballon d'Or winner, Modrić led Croatia to their first World Cup final. He has won multiple Champions League titles with Real Madrid.
