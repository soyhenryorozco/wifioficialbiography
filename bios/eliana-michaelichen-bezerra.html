Eliana Michaelichen Bezerra (born November 22, 1973), known as Eliana Michaelichen Bezerra, is a brazilian tv host. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Eliana is a Brazilian TV host and actress, one of Brazil's most popular entertainers. She has hosted numerous variety shows for decades.
