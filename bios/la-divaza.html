La Divaza (born January 3, 1999), known as La Divaza, is a venezuelan youtuber and entertainer. Born in Maracaibo, Venezuela, La Divaza is one of Venezuela's most famous YouTubers, known for comedy sketches and lifestyle content. Despite leaving Venezuela, they remain a cultural reference for Latin American youth.
