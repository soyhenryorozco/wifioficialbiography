Jude Bellingham (born June 29, 2003), known as Jude Bellingham, is a english footballer. Born in Stourbridge, England, Jude Bellingham became one of the youngest stars in Real Madrid history after his record-breaking transfer from Borussia Dortmund, immediately winning La Liga in his debut season.
