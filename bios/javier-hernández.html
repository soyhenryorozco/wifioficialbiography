Javier Hernández (born June 1, 1988), known as Javier Hernández, is a mexican professional footballer. Born in Guadalajara, Mexico, Known as Chicharito, he is Mexico's all-time leading scorer. He won titles with Manchester United and Real Madrid before playing for LA Galaxy.
