Gael García Bernal (born November 30, 1978), known as Gael García Bernal, is a mexican actor. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Gael García Bernal is a Mexican actor and director known for Amores Perros, Y Tu Mamá También, and The Motorcycle Diaries.
