Edgar Davids (born March 13, 1973), known as Edgar Davids, is a dutch footballer. Born in Paramaribo, Suriname, Edgar Davids was a tenacious midfielder who starred for Ajax, Juventus, and Barcelona, recognizable by his distinctive protective glasses and dreadlocks.
