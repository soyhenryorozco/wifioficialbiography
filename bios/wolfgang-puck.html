Wolfgang Puck (born July 8, 1949), known as Wolfgang Puck, is a austrian-american chef and restaurateur. Born in Sankt Veit an der Glan, Austria, Wolfgang Puck is an Austrian-American chef and restaurateur known for his fine dining and celebrity catering.
