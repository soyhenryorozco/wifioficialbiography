Gloria Groove (born January 18, 1995), known as Gloria Groove, is a brazilian singer. Born in Brasília, Brazil, Gloria Groove is a Brazilian singer known for her powerful voice and fusion of pop, funk, and electronic music. She has become one of Brazil's biggest stars.
