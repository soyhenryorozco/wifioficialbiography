Los Kjarkas (born 1965), known as Los Kjarkas, is a bolivian folk band. Born in Cochabamba, Bolivia, Los Kjarkas are a Bolivian folk group known for preserving Andean music. Their song Llorando se Fue became the basis for the worldwide hit Lambada.
