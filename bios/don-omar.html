Don Omar (born February 10, 1978), known as Don Omar, is a puerto rican singer. Born in Santurce, Puerto Rico, Don Omar is a Puerto Rican reggaeton singer known as El Rey. He is one of the genre's pioneers with hits like Dile and the Fast & Furious franchise.
