María Gabriela de Faría (born September 11, 1992), known as María Gabriela de Faría, is a venezuelan actress. Born in Caracas, Venezuela, María Gabriela de Faría is a Venezuelan actress and singer known for telenovelas and the Netflix series The Exorcist: Believer.
