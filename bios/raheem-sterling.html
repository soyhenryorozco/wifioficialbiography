Raheem Sterling (born December 8, 1994), known as Raheem Sterling, is a english footballer. Born in Kingston, Jamaica, Raheem Sterling has been a key player for Manchester City and Chelsea, winning multiple Premier League titles and becoming an outspoken advocate for racial equality in football.
