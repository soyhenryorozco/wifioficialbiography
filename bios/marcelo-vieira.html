Marcelo Vieira (born May 12, 1988), known as Marcelo Vieira, is a brazilian footballer. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Marcelo is Real Madrid's most decorated player with five Champions League titles, known for his extraordinary attacking ability from the left-back position.
