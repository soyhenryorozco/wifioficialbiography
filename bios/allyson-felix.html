Allyson Felix (born November 18, 1985), known as Allyson Felix, is a american sprinter. Born in Los Angeles, California, USA, Allyson Felix is the most decorated American track and field athlete in Olympic history with 11 medals, becoming a vocal advocate for maternal rights in sports.
