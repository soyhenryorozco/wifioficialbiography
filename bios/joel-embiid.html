Joel Embiid (born March 16, 1994), known as Joel Embiid, is a cameroonian basketball player. Born in Yaoundé, Cameroon, Joel Embiid won the 2023 NBA MVP award and has become one of the most dominant two-way centers in the league with the Philadelphia 76ers.
