Juan Luis Guerra (born June 7, 1957), known as Juan Luis Guerra, is a dominican singer. Born in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, Juan Luis Guerra is a Dominican singer known for merengue and bachata music. He has won multiple Grammy and Latin Grammy Awards.
