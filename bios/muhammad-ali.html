Muhammad Ali (born January 17, 1942), known as Muhammad Ali, is a american boxer and activist. Born in Louisville, Kentucky, USA, Muhammad Ali is widely regarded as the greatest boxer of all time, a three-time heavyweight champion whose charisma and activism transcended the sport.
