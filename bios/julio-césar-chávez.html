Julio César Chávez (born February 12, 1962), known as Julio César Chávez, is a mexican professional boxer. Born in Ciudad Obregón, Mexico, Considered the greatest Mexican boxer of all time, Chávez held world titles in three weight divisions. He retired with an incredible 107-6 record.
