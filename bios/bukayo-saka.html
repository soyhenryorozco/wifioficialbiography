Bukayo Saka (born September 5, 2001), known as Bukayo Saka, is a english footballer. Born in London, England, Bukayo Saka has become Arsenal's talisman and one of the Premier League's best players, overcoming his penalty heartbreak at Euro 2020 to become a world-class talent.
