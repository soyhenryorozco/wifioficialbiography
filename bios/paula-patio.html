Public Figure Latin America

Paula Andrea Patiño Bedoya (born 29 March 1997) is a Colombian racing cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Women's WorldTeam Movistar Team. She rode in the women's road race event at the 2018 UCI Road World Championships.

Career

Patiño won Stage 2 of the Vuelta a Colombia Femenina in 2018. She competed at the 2020 Summer Olympics.
