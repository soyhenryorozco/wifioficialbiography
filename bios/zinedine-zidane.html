Zinedine Zidane (born June 23, 1972), known as Zinedine Zidane, is a french professional footballer. Born in Marseille, France, One of the greatest midfielders in football history, Zidane won the 1998 World Cup and Euro 2000 with France. He later became a highly successful manager at Real Madrid.
