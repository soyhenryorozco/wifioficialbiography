Noel Gallagher (born May 29, 1967), known as Noel Gallagher, is a british singer and songwriter. Born in Manchester, United Kingdom, Noel Gallagher is the co-lead vocalist and primary songwriter of Oasis, one of the biggest Britpop bands.
