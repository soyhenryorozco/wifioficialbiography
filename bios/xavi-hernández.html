Xavi Hernández (born January 25, 1980), known as Xavi Hernández, is a spanish professional footballer. Born in Terrassa, Spain, Widely regarded as one of the greatest midfielders of all time, Xavi won the 2010 World Cup and two European Championships. He became head coach of FC Barcelona.
