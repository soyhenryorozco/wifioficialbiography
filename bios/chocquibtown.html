ChocQuibTown is a Colombian hip hop group formed in Quibdó, Chocó. The group consists of Tostao, Lojo, and Slow. They are known for blending hip hop with traditional Afro-Colombian music like cumbia, bullerengue, and mapalé.

Their music addresses social issues and celebrates Afro-Colombian culture. They have won multiple Latin Grammy Awards and are considered pioneers of the Colombian urban music movement.
