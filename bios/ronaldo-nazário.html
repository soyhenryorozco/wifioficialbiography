Ronaldo Nazário (born September 18, 1976), known as Ronaldo Nazário, is a brazilian professional footballer. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Known as O Fenômeno, Ronaldo is considered one of the greatest strikers ever. He won two World Cups with Brazil and three FIFA World Player of the Year awards.
