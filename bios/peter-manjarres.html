Peter Jiménez Manjarrés (born January 7, 1958) is a Colombian vallenato singer and composer. Born in Valledupar, Cesar, he is one of the most important figures in modern vallenato.

Manjarrés has recorded numerous hits including 'La rough y la Vaca', 'Cosas del Alma', and 'El Ausente'. He has won multiple Latin Grammy awards and is a key figure in the Festival de la Leyenda Vallenata.
