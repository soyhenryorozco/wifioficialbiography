Ángel Di María (born February 14, 1988), known as Ángel Di María, is a argentine professional footballer. Born in Rosario, Argentina, Di María scored the winning goal in the 2024 Copa América final for Argentina. He has won the Champions League with Real Madrid and titles across Europe's top leagues.
