Nicki Minaj (born December 8, 1982), known as Nicki Minaj, is a trinidadian-american rapper and singer. Born in Saint James, Trinidad and Tobago, Nicki Minaj is the best-selling female rapper of all time, known for her colorful persona and rapid-fire flow.
