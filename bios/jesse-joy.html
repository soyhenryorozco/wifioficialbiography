Jesse & Joy (born June 5, 1986), known as Jesse & Joy, is a mexican pop duo. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Jesse & Joy are a Mexican pop duo known for emotional ballads and heartfelt lyrics. They have won multiple Latin Grammy Awards.
