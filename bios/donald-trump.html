Donald Trump (born June 14, 1946), known as Donald Trump, is a american former president of the united states. Born in Queens, New York, USA, Donald Trump served as the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021. Before politics, he was a real estate mogul and television personality on The Apprentice.
