Bernard Arnault (born March 5, 1949), known as Bernard Arnault, is a french ceo of lvmh. Born in Roubaix, France, Bernard Arnault is the chairman and CEO of LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton, the world's largest luxury goods company.
