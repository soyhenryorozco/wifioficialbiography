Venus Williams (born June 17, 1980), known as Venus Williams, is a american tennis player. Born in Lynwood, California, USA, Venus Williams won seven Grand Slam singles titles and was a pioneer for equal prize money in tennis, also excelling as an entrepreneur and designer.
