Dylan Mulvaney

Dylan Mulvaney

Dylan Mulvaney is an American social media personality, actress, and singer who is best known for detailing her gender transition in daily videos published on TikTok since early 2022. Before coming out as a transgender woman and launching her internet career, Mulvaney performed as a stage actor in Broadway national tours and in Off-Broadway and Old Globe Theatre productions. She gained a higher profile on social media platforms after her interview with U.S. president Joe Biden at the White House in October 2022, during which they spoke about transgender rights. After Bud Light sent a beer can with her face printed on it to Mulvaney for an Instagram promotion in 2023, American conservatives led a boycott of the brand.
