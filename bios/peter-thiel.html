Peter Thiel (born October 11, 1967), known as Peter Thiel, is a german-american entrepreneur and venture capitalist. Born in Frankfurt, West Germany, Peter Thiel is a venture capitalist, entrepreneur, and author who co-founded PayPal and Palantir Technologies. He was an early investor in Facebook.
