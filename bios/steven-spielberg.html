Steven Spielberg (born December 18, 1946), known as Steven Spielberg, is a american film director and producer. Born in Cincinnati, Ohio, USA, Steven Spielberg is one of the most influential film directors in history, known for classics like Jaws, E.T., Jurassic Park, and Schindler's List.
