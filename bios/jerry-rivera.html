Jerry Rivera (born July 31, 1973), known as Jerry Rivera, is a puerto rican singer. Born in Humacao, Puerto Rico, Jerry Rivera is a Puerto Rican salsa singer who became famous as a teenager. He is known as El Bebé de la Salsa and has had numerous hit albums.
