Narendra Modi (born September 17, 1950), known as Narendra Modi, is a indian prime minister of india. Born in Vadnagar, Gujarat, India, Narendra Modi has served as Prime Minister of India since 2014. He previously served as Chief Minister of Gujarat and is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party.
