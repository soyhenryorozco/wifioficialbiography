Paolo Maldini (born June 26, 1968), known as Paolo Maldini, is a italian professional footballer. Born in Milan, Italy, A legendary defender who spent his entire career at AC Milan, Maldini won five Champions League titles. He is regarded as one of the greatest defenders ever.
