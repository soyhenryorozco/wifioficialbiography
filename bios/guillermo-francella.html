Guillermo Francella (born February 14, 1955), known as Guillermo Francella, is a argentine actor. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Guillermo Francella is an Argentine actor and comedian known for his roles in Argentine films and television. He is one of Argentina's most beloved entertainers.
