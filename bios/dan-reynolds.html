Dan Reynolds (born July 14, 1987), known as Dan Reynolds, is a american singer and songwriter. Born in Nevada City, United States, Dan Reynolds is the lead singer of Imagine Dragons, known for anthemic hits like 'Radioactive' and 'Believer'.
