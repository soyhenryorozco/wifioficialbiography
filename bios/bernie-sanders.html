Bernie Sanders (born September 8, 1941), known as Bernie Sanders, is a american u.s. senator. Born in Brooklyn, New York, USA, Bernie Sanders is a U.S. Senator from Vermont and a prominent progressive voice in American politics. He ran for President in 2016 and 2020.
