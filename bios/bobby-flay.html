Bobby Flay (born December 10, 1964), known as Bobby Flay, is a american chef and television personality. Born in New York City, New York, USA, Bobby Flay is a celebrity chef and Food Network star known for shows like Bobby Flay's Throwdown and Beat Bobby Flay.
