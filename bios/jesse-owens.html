Jesse Owens (born September 12, 1913), known as Jesse Owens, is a american sprinter. Born in Oakville, Alabama, USA, Jesse Owens won four gold medals at the 1936 Berlin Olympics, defying Adolf Hitler's ideology of Aryan supremacy and becoming a civil rights icon.
