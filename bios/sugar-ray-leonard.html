Sugar Ray Leonard (born May 17, 1956), known as Sugar Ray Leonard, is a american boxer. Born in Wilmington, North Carolina, USA, Sugar Ray Leonard was one of the Four Kings of boxing in the 1980s, winning world titles in five weight divisions and the 1976 Olympic gold medal.
