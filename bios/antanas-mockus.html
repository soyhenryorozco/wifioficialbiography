Antanas Mockus Šivickas (born March 25, 1952) is a Colombian politician and mathematician. Born in Bogotá to Lithuanian parents, he served twice as mayor of Bogotá.

Mockus is known for his creative and unconventional approach to governance, using art and humor to address social issues.
