Lucas Bravo

Lucas Nicolas Joseph Bravo

Lucas Nicolas Bravo (French: [lykɑ bʁavo]; born 26 March 1988) is a French actor. He is best known for starring in the Netflix romantic comedy series Emily in Paris (2020–present).
