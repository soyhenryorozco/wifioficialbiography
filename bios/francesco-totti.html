Francesco Totti (born September 27, 1976), known as Francesco Totti, is a italian footballer. Born in Rome, Italy, Francesco Totti spent his entire 25-year career at AS Roma, becoming the club's all-time top scorer and a symbol of loyalty in Italian football.
