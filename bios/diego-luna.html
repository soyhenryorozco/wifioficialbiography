Diego Luna (born December 29, 1979), known as Diego Luna, is a mexican actor. Born in Mexico City, Mexico, Diego Luna is a Mexican actor and director known for Rogue One: A Star Wars Story and the series Andor. He bridges Mexican and Hollywood cinema.
