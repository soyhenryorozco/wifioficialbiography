Andrea Serna

Colombian TV host, journalist and model.

TV HostColombiaJournalistModel

Andrea Serna is a Colombian TV host, journalist, and model. She has been one of the most prominent television presenters in Colombia, hosting various entertainment and news programs.

Serna is known for her work on Noticias RCN and Caracol Televisión, where she has hosted major entertainment shows and events including Miss Universe broadcasts.
