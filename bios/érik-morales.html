Érik Morales (born January 1, 1976), known as Érik Morales, is a mexican professional boxer. Born in Tijuana, Mexico, Known as 'El Terrible', Morales won world titles in four weight divisions. He is famous for his epic trilogy of fights against Marco Antonio Barrera.
