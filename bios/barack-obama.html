Barack Obama (born August 4, 1961), known as Barack Obama, is a american former president of the united states. Born in Honolulu, Hawaii, USA, Barack Obama served as the 44th President of the United States from 2009 to 2017, becoming the first African American to hold the office. He was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2009.
