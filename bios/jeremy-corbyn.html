Jeremy Corbyn (born May 26, 1949), known as Jeremy Corbyn, is a british former leader of the labour party. Born in Chippenham, England, UK, Jeremy Corbyn served as Leader of the Labour Party and Leader of the Opposition from 2015 to 2020. He is a lifelong anti-war activist and socialist.
