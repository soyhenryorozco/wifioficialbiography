Julio Jaramillo (born October 1, 1935), known as Julio Jaramillo, is a ecuadorian singer. Born in Guayaquil, Ecuador, Julio Jaramillo was an Ecuadorian singer known as one of the greatest bolero singers in Latin American history. His music transcended borders.
