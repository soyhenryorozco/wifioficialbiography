Elaine Thompson-Herah (born June 28, 1992), known as Elaine Thompson-Herah, is a jamaican sprinter. Born in Manchester, Jamaica, Elaine Thompson-Herah completed the Olympic sprint double-double by winning both the 100m and 200m at the 2016 and 2020 Olympics.
