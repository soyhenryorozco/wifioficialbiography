Leonardo DiCaprio (born November 11, 1974), known as Leonardo DiCaprio, is a american actor and producer. Born in Los Angeles, California, USA, Leonardo DiCaprio is an acclaimed actor who won an Oscar for The Revenant. He is also a prominent environmental activist.
