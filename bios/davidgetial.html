Davidgetial (David Getial) is a Colombian tiktok star, digital influencer, digital artist. Colombian TikToker famous for fashion poses, comedy sketches, and iPad digital art drawings of celebrities and pop culture icons.

Early life and background

David Getial was born on March 21, 2000, in Colombia. He started creating content combining fashion, comedy, and digital art on his iPad.

Career

Davidgetial is a Colombian TikTok star famous for his creative blend of fashion poses, comedy sketches, and digital art drawings on iPad. His digital art consists of drawings of celebrities and pop culture icons.

Ranked #33 in the B-Fun 2025 Digital Influence Index with 14M+ TikTok followers and 376M+ total likes, he has built a unique niche combining multiple content forms.

His viral Rihanna fan video accumulated 5M+ views, and he continues to grow his audience through innovative content that blends fashion, comedy, and digital artistry.
