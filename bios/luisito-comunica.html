Luis Arturo Villar Sudek (born March 20, 1991), known as Luisito Comunica, is a Mexican YouTuber, actor, voice actor, influencer, and entrepreneur. Born in Puebla de Zaragoza, he is one of the most popular content creators in Latin America.

His YouTube channel has over 45 million subscribers and more than 33 million followers on Instagram. He is the fifth most-subscribed channel in Mexico.

Luisito has also ventured into acting, dubbing, and entrepreneurship. He has been recognized as one of the most influential digital creators in Latin America.
