Dustin Poirier (born January 19, 1989), known as Dustin Poirier, is a american mma fighter. Born in Lafayette, Louisiana, USA, Dustin Poirier is one of the UFC's most beloved fighters, known for his exciting fighting style and charitable work through the Good Fight Foundation.
