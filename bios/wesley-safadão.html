Wesley Safadão (born September 28, 1988), known as Wesley Safadão, is a brazilian singer. Born in Fortaleza, Brazil, Wesley Safadão is a Brazilian singer known for forró and sertanejo music. He is one of the biggest names in Brazilian music with sold-out shows nationwide.
