River Butcher

River Buddy Butcher (formerly Rhea Harriett Butcher; born August 12, 1982) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, writer, producer, and podcast host. He is best known for personal, observational comedy focused on his vegetarianism, feminism, love of baseball, and experiences as a queer person.
