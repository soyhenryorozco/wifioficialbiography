Imran Khan (born October 5, 1952), known as Imran Khan, is a pakistani former prime minister of pakistan. Born in Lahore, Pakistan, Imran Khan served as Prime Minister of Pakistan from 2018 to 2022. Before politics, he was a legendary cricketer who captained Pakistan to World Cup victory in 1992.
