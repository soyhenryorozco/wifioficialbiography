A-Lee (born May 17, 1988) is a pop recording artist. Ali Pirzad-Amoli (Persian: علی پیرزاد آملی; born 17 May 1988), better known by his stage name A-Lee (also sometimes stylized as A-LEE), is a Norwegian pop recording artist, rapper, singer, songwriter

A-Lee is a multi-gold and platinum selling artist who released his debut album Missing in 2010 and his second album Forever Lost in 2012 which contains numerous chart topping songs. His singles "The One" and "World So Cold" reached respectively #7 and #12 at Norwegian Official Charts VG-Lista.A-Lee

During his career, A-Lee made many appearances on Norwegian TV (MTV Norway, national channels NRK & TV2, The Voice TV), extensive Norwegian radio (NRJ Radio, The Voice, NRK P3, NRK mP3, P4, P5, JRG Group). A-Lee performed more than 300 concerts, including as support for Tinie Tempah, 50 Cent, Snoop
