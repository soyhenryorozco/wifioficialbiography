Eddie Murphy (born April 3, 1961), known as Eddie Murphy, is a american comedian and actor. Born in Brooklyn, New York, USA, Eddie Murphy is a legendary comedian who rose to fame on Saturday Night Live and starred in Beverly Hills Cop and Coming to America.
