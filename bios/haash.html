Ha*Ash (born June 27, 1985), known as Ha*Ash, is a american-mexican country pop duo. Born in Lake Charles, United States, Ha*Ash is an American-Mexican country pop duo consisting of sisters Hanna and Ashley Nicole Pérez. They have become one of the top Latin American country music acts.
