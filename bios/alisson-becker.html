Alisson Becker (born October 2, 1992), known as Alisson Becker, is a brazilian footballer. Born in Novo Hamburgo, Brazil, Alisson Becker won the Champions League, Premier League, and Copa America in the same calendar year, establishing himself as one of the best goalkeepers in world football.
