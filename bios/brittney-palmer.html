Brittney Palmer

Brittney Palmer

Brittney Palmer is an American former UFC Octagon Girl, model and artist. She worked as a ring card girl for World Extreme Cagefighting and later the Ultimate Fighting Championship. Palmer received the World MMA Award for Ringcard Girl of the Year six times, and announced her retirement from UFC appearances in December 2023, with UFC 296 serving as her final event. She has also worked as a contemporary artist, with portraits and abstract paintings exhibited in Los Angeles, Miami, New York, Hong Kong, and Milan.
