David Alonso (born 2006-04-25) is a Colombian piloto de motociclismo.

David Alonso Gómez (born 25 April 2006) is a Grand Prix motorcycle racer who currently competes for the CFMoto Aspar Racing Team in the Moto2 World Championship. Colombian by maternal descent and Spanish by paternal descent, he represents Colombia internationally. Born in Spain, he also has Spanish citizenship. He won the European Talent Cup in 2020 and the Red Bull MotoGP Rookies Cup in 2021.
