Linus Tech Tips (born 1986-08-25), known as Linus Tech Tips, is a canadian tech youtuber. Born in Vancouver, Canada, Linus Sebastian runs Linus Tech Tips, one of the largest tech review channels on YouTube covering hardware, software, and tech news.
