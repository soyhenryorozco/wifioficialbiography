Mike Bahía (born September 17, 1987), known as Mike Bahía, is a colombian singer. Born in Cali, Colombia, Mike Bahía is a Colombian pop and tropical singer known for hits like Ella y Yo and La Cara Nueva. He blends pop with tropical rhythms.
