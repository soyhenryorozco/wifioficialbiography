Vinícius Júnior (born July 12, 2000), known as Vinícius Júnior, is a brazilian professional footballer. Born in São Gonçalo, Brazil, Vinícius scored the winning goal in the 2024 Champions League final for Real Madrid. He is known for his explosive speed and dribbling ability.
