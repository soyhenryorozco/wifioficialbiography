Davinson Sánchez (born June 12, 1996), known as Davinson Sánchez, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Caloto, Colombia, Sánchez moved from Atlético Nacional to Ajax and then Tottenham, becoming a key central defender. He won the Copa Libertadores before his European career.
