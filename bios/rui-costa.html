Rui Costa (born March 29, 1972), known as Rui Costa, is a portuguese footballer and executive. Born in Lisbon, Portugal, Rui Costa was one of the most elegant attacking midfielders of his generation, starring for Fiorentina and AC Milan before becoming Benfica's president.
