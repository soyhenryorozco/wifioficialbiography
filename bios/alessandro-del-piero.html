Alessandro Del Piero (born November 9, 1974), known as Alessandro Del Piero, is a italian footballer. Born in Conegliano, Italy, Alessandro Del Piero is Juventus' all-time leading scorer, known for his elegant playing style, free-kick ability, and winning the 2006 World Cup with Italy.
