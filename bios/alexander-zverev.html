Alexander Zverev (born April 20, 1997), known as Alexander Zverev, is a german tennis player. Born in Hamburg, Germany, Alexander Zverev won the ATP Finals at age 21 and Olympic gold in Tokyo, establishing himself among the elite of men's tennis.
