Khloe Kardashian (born June 27, 1984), known as Khloe Kardashian, is a american reality tv star and businesswoman. Born in Los Angeles, California, USA, Khloe Kardashian is a reality television star known for Keeping Up with the Kardashians. She founded the fashion brand Good American.
