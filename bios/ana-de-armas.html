Ana Celia de Armas Caso (born April 30, 1988), known as Ana de Armas, is a Cuban-Spanish actress. Born in La Habana, Cuba, she began her career at 16 in Cuban cinema before moving to Spain and then Hollywood.

De Armas gained recognition for the Spanish TV series El internado (2007-2010). After relocating to Los Angeles, she appeared in Blade Runner 2049 (2017) and Knives Out (2019).

She received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress for her portrayal of Marilyn Monroe in Blonde (2022). She also starred as Paloma in No Time to Die (2021).
