Israel Adesanya (born July 22, 1989), known as Israel Adesanya, is a new zealander mma fighter. Born in Lagos, Nigeria, Israel Adesanya, known as The Last Stylebender, became the UFC middleweight champion with his exceptional kickboxing skills and charismatic personality.
