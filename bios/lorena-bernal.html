Lorena Bernal (born May 1, 1981), known as Lorena Bernal, is a argentine-spanish actress. Born in San Juan, Argentina, Lorena Bernal is an Argentine-Spanish actress, model, and TV host. She was crowned Miss Spain and has appeared in Spanish television productions.
