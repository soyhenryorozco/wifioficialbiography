Natasha Alexandra Klauss Rentería (born July 2, 1983) is a Colombian actress and model. Born in Cali, Valle del Cauca, she is known for her roles in popular telenovelas.

Klauss gained fame for her role in Sin senos sí hay paraíso and other Colombian television productions. She has become one of the most recognized faces in Colombian television.
