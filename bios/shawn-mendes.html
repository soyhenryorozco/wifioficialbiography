Shawn Mendes (born August 8, 1998), known as Shawn Mendes, is a canadian singer-songwriter. Born in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, Shawn Mendes rose from Vine to pop stardom with hits like Stitches and There's Nothing Holdin' Me Back.
