Gareth Bale (born July 16, 1989), known as Gareth Bale, is a welsh footballer. Born in Cardiff, Wales, Gareth Bale became the world's most expensive player when he moved to Real Madrid, winning five Champions League titles and scoring iconic goals including in two finals.
