Christian Nodal (born January 11, 1999), known as Christian Nodal, is a mexican singer. Born in Caborca, Mexico, Christian Nodal is a Mexican singer known for his mariachi and norteño music. He burst onto the scene with Adiós Amor and has won multiple Latin Grammy Awards.
