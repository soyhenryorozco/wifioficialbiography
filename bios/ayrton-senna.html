Ayrton Senna (born March 21, 1960), known as Ayrton Senna, is a brazilian formula one driver. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Ayrton Senna won three Formula One World Championships and is revered as one of the greatest drivers in history, tragically killed at Imola in 1994.
