Vladimir Putin (born October 7, 1952), known as Vladimir Putin, is a russian president of russia. Born in Leningrad, Soviet Union, Vladimir Putin has been President of Russia since 2000, with a brief stint as Prime Minister. He is the longest-serving Russian leader since Joseph Stalin.
