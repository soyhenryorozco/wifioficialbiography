Jensen Huang (born February 17, 1963), known as Jensen Huang, is a taiwanese-american ceo of nvidia. Born in Tainan, Taiwan, Jensen Huang co-founded NVIDIA and serves as its CEO, leading the company to become the dominant player in AI chips and graphics processing units.
