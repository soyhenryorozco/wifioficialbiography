Luis Díaz (born January 13, 1997) is a Colombian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Liverpool and the Colombia national team.

Díaz rose to prominence at Porto before joining Liverpool in January 2022. He has been a key player for Colombia, helping the team reach the 2024 Copa América final.
