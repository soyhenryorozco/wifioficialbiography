Ronaldinho (born March 21, 1980), known as Ronaldinho, is a brazilian footballer. Born in Porto Alegre, Brazil, Ronaldinho is celebrated for his extraordinary creativity, dribbling skills, and joy on the pitch, winning two FIFA World Player of theYear awards and the 2002 World Cup with Brazil.
