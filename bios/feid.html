Feid (born August 19, 1992), also known as Ferxxo, is a Colombian singer, songwriter, and record producer. A Paisa born Salomón Villada Hoyos in Medellín in 1992, he takes pride in his roots and decorates his romantic lyrics with local slang. He's also a musical adventurer who spikes his otherwise classic perreo with dark, chilly synths and the moody energy of R&B.

Feid's breakout came with the 2022 album "FELIZ CUMPLEAÑOS FERXXO TE PIRATEAMOS EL ÁLBUM", which saw him experimenting with electronic genres and cutting tech-house bangers. By the end of 2023, he had teamed up with Bad Bunny on "PERRO NEGRO", which scored them both a Latin Grammy for Best Reggaeton Performance.

Early in his career, Feid wrote songs for other artists including J Balvin and Reykon. His versatility has been demonstrated through collaborations with artists like Sky Rompiendo and Japanese rapper Yuki Chiba.
