Margarita Rosa de Francisco Baquero (born August 24, 1963) is a Colombian actress and singer. Born in Cali, Valle del Cauca, she was crowned Miss Colombia 1983.

De Francisco became one of the most beloved actresses in Colombian television history, starring in numerous popular telenovelas.
