Eduardo Vargas (born November 20, 1989), known as Eduardo Vargas, is a chilean professional footballer. Born in Santiago, Chile, Vargas scored the winning goal in the 2015 Copa América final for Chile. He has played for Napoli, QPR, and Atlético Mineiro in a versatile forward role.
