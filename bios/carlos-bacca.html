Carlos Bacca (born September 1, 1986), known as Carlos Bacca, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Puerto Boyacá, Colombia, Bacca won the Europa League with Sevilla and later played for AC Milan. He scored two goals in the 2015 Copa América for Colombia.
