Ronald Acuña Jr. (born June 18, 1997), known as Ronald Acuña Jr., is a venezuelan baseball player. Born in La Guaira, Venezuela, Ronald Acuña Jr. became the first player to record 40 HR and 70 stolen bases in a season.
