Cafu (born June 7, 1970), known as Cafu, is a brazilian footballer. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, Cafu captained Brazil to victory in the 2002 World Cup and appeared in three consecutive World Cup finals, making him one of the greatest right-backs ever.
