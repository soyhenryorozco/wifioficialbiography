Manu Ginóbili (born July 28, 1977), known as Manu Ginóbili, is a argentine professional basketball player. Born in Bahía Blanca, Argentina, A four-time NBA champion with the San Antonio Spurs, Ginóbili is considered the greatest Latin American basketball player ever. He also won Olympic gold in 2004.
