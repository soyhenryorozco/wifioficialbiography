Simone & Simaria (born December 15, 1982), known as Simone & Simaria, is a brazilian sertanejo duo. Born in Salvador, Brazil, Simone & Simaria were a Brazilian sertanejo duo known for powerful harmonies and romantic songs. They were among Brazil's most popular duos.
