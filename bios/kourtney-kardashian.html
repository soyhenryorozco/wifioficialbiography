Kourtney Kardashian (born April 18, 1979), known as Kourtney Kardashian, is a american reality tv star and businesswoman. Born in Los Angeles, California, USA, Kourtney Kardashian is a reality television star known for Keeping Up with the Kardashians. She founded the lifestyle brand Poosh.
