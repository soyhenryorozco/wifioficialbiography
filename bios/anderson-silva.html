Anderson Silva (born April 14, 1975), known as Anderson Silva, is a brazilian mma fighter. Born in Curitiba, Brazil, Anderson Silva holds the record for the longest UFC title reign at 2,457 days, dominating the middleweight division for nearly a decade with his creative striking.
