Chino & Nacho (born June 4, 1983), known as Chino & Nacho, is a venezuelan pop duo. Born in Caracas, Venezuela, Chino & Nacho are a Venezuelan pop duo known for catchy songs and romantic lyrics. They have won a Latin Grammy Award.
