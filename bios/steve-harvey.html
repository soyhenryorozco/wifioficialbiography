Steve Harvey (born January 17, 1957), known as Steve Harvey, is a american comedian and talk show host. Born in Welch, West Virginia, USA, Steve Harvey is a comedian and television host who hosts Family Feud and The Steve Harvey Show. He also hosted the Miss Universe pageant.
