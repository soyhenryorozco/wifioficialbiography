Jacksepticeye (born February 7, 1990), known as Jacksepticeye, is a irish youtuber and gaming personality. Born in Athlone, County Westmeath, Ireland, Jacksepticeye, born Sean McLoughlin, is an Irish gaming YouTuber known for his high-energy Let's Play videos. He founded the charity clothing line Cloak.
