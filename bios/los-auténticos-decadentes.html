Los Auténticos Decadentes (born 1986), known as Los Auténticos Decadentes, is a argentine rock band. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Los Auténticos Decadentes are an Argentine rock band known for their cumbia-rock fusion. Their song La Guitarra is an anthem across Latin America.
