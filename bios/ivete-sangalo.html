Ivete Sangalo (born May 17, 1972), known as Ivete Sangalo, is a brazilian singer. Born in Salvador, Brazil, Ivete Sangalo is a Brazilian singer known as the Queen of Axé. She has sold over 20 million records and is one of Brazil's most beloved entertainers.
