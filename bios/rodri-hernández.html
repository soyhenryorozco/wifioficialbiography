Rodri Hernández (born June 22, 1996), known as Rodri Hernández, is a spanish professional footballer. Born in Madrid, Spain, Rodri won Euro 2024 with Spain and the Champions League with Manchester City. He was awarded the 2024 Ballon d'Or for his outstanding midfield performances.
