Angela Merkel (born July 17, 1954), known as Angela Merkel, is a german former chancellor of germany. Born in Hamburg, West Germany, Angela Merkel served as Chancellor of Germany from 2005 to 2021, leading the country for 16 years. She was widely regarded as the de facto leader of the European Union.
