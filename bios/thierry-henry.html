Thierry Henry (born August 17, 1977), known as Thierry Henry, is a french footballer and pundit. Born in Les Ulis, France, Thierry Henry is Arsenal's all-time leading scorer and a 1998 World Cup winner with France, celebrated for his pace, elegance, and clinical finishing.
