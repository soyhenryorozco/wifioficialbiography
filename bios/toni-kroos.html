Toni Kroos (born January 4, 1990), known as Toni Kroos, is a german footballer. Born in Greifswald, Germany, Toni Kroos is one of the most decorated midfielders in football history, winning six Champions League titles with Real Madrid and the 2014 World Cup with Germany.
