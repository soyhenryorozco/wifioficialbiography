Carolina Gaitán Lozano (born May 20, 1990) is a Colombian actress and singer. Born in Bogotá, she is the Spanish voice of Pepa Madrigal in Disney's animated film Encanto.

Gaitán has also starred in Colombian telenovelas and has a growing international career.
