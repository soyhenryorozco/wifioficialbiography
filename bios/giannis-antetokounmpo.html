Giannis Antetokounmpo (born December 6, 1994), known as Giannis Antetokounmpo, is a greek basketball player. Born in Athens, Greece, Giannis Antetokounmpo, known as the Greek Freak, led the Milwaukee Bucks to their first championship in 50 years in 2021 and won back-to-back MVP awards.
