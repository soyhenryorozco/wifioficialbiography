Sadio Mane (born April 10, 1992), known as Sadio Mane, is a senegalese footballer. Born in Bambali, Senegal, Sadio Mane won the Champions League with Liverpool and the Africa Cup of Nations with Senegal, establishing himself as one of Africa's greatest ever players.
