Huda Kattan (born 1983-10-02), known as Huda Kattan, is a american-iraqi beauty influencer and entrepreneur. Born in Oklahoma City, USA, Huda Kattan is the founder of Huda Beauty and one of the most influential beauty influencers in the world.
