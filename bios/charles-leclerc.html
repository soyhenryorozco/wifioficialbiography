Charles Leclerc (born October 16, 1997), known as Charles Leclerc, is a monegasque formula one driver. Born in Monte Carlo, Monaco, Charles Leclerc is Ferrari's star driver and a multiple race winner, widely tipped as a future Formula One World Champion.
