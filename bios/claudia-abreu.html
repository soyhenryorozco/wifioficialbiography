Claudia Abreu (born July 12, 1968), known as Claudia Abreu, is a brazilian actress. Born in Salvador, Brazil, Claudia Abreu is a Brazilian actress known for roles in films and telenovelas. She has been a prominent figure in Brazilian entertainment for decades.
