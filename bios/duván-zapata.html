Duván Zapata (born April 1, 1991), known as Duván Zapata, is a colombian professional footballer. Born in Cali, Colombia, Zapata has been a prolific striker for Napoli, Atalanta, and Torino in Serie A. His powerful finishing and aerial ability make him one of Colombia's top forwards.
