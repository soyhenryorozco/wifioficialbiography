Taís Araújo (born November 25, 1978), known as Taís Araújo, is a brazilian actress. Born in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, Taís Araújo is a Brazilian actress known as the first Black leading actress in a Brazilian telenovela. She has been a trailblazer for representation.
