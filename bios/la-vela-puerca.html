La Vela Puerca (born 1995), known as La Vela Puerca, is a uruguayan ska punk band. Born in Montevideo, Uruguay, La Vela Puerca is a Uruguayan ska punk band known for energetic live shows and socially conscious lyrics. They are one of South America's most popular rock bands.
