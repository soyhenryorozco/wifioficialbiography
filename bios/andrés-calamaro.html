Andrés Calamaro (born August 22, 1961), known as Andrés Calamaro, is a argentine singer. Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Andrés Calamaro is an Argentine rock musician considered one of the most important figures in Latin American rock. He was a member of Los Abuelos de la Nada.
