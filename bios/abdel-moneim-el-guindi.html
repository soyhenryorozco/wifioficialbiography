Public Figure Latin America

Abdel Moneim El-Guindi (Egyptian Arabic: عبد المنعم الجندى; 12 June 1936 – 17 March 2011) was an Egyptian amateur flyweight boxer. He competed for the United Arab Republic at the 1960 Summer Olympics and won a bronze medal.
