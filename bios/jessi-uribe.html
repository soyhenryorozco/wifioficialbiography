Jessi Uribe is a Colombian singer of popular music, known for romantic hits like Dulce Pecado and Repítela. He has become one of the most popular Colombian artists in the regional Mexican and popular music genres.

His music combines traditional Colombian popular music with modern romantic ballad influences, creating a distinctive style that has earned him millions of fans across Latin America and the United States.

Early life

Jessi Uribe was born on March 20, 1995, in Bucaramanga, Colombia. From a young age, he showed a passion for music, influenced by the popular music traditions of Colombia. He began singing in local events and festivals, developing his vocal skills and stage presence.

Career

Jessi Uribe launched his professional music career in the mid-2010s, quickly establishing himself as a rising star in the Colombian popular music scene. His breakthrough came with the hit single Dulce Pecado, which garnered millions of streams and established him as a leading voice in the genre.

He followed up with other successful singles including Repítela, Te Olvidé, and collaborations with other prominent artists. His music explores themes of love, heartbreak, and romance, resonating with audiences across Latin America.

Uribe has performed at major venues and festivals throughout Colombia, Mexico, and the United States, building a reputation as a compelling live performer.
